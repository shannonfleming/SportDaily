**Leeds United secured their spot in the FA Cup semi-finals after a thrilling match against West Ham, ending in a 2-2 draw and a 4-2 win in the penalty shoot-out.**

## Context / Background
The FA Cup has been a platform for teams to showcase their skills and determination. This match between West Ham and Leeds United was highly anticipated, with both teams looking to advance to the semi-finals.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The match saw both teams create scoring opportunities, with West Ham initially taking the lead. However, Leeds United fought back, equalizing and taking the match to a penalty shoot-out. The shoot-out was chaotic, with Leeds United ultimately emerging victorious.

> The win feels fantastic, and we're looking forward to the semi-finals, said Daniel Farke, Leeds United's coach.

## What's Next?
Leeds United will now face their next opponent in the FA Cup semi-finals, looking to continue their winning streak. The team's determination and skill have taken them this far, and they're eager to see how far they can go in the competition.
