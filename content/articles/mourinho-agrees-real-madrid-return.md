**Jose Mourinho has agreed to return to Real Madrid as head coach on a two-year deal, marking his second stint with the Spanish giants.**

## Context / Background
Mourinho previously managed Real Madrid from 2010 to 2013, winning one La Liga title and one Copa del Rey. His return to the club has been highly anticipated, and the two-year deal is set to bring stability to the team.

## Tactical Key Points
Mourinho's tactical approach has always been focused on discipline and organization. His ability to adapt to different teams and leagues has been key to his success. With Real Madrid, he will be looking to impose his style on the team and bring back the success that the club is accustomed to.

> Mourinho's experience and tactical acumen make him an ideal candidate to lead Real Madrid back to the top of Spanish football.

## What's Next?
Mourinho's first match in charge of Real Madrid is yet to be announced, but fans are eagerly awaiting his return to the dugout. With the new season approaching, all eyes will be on the Portuguese manager to see how he will fare in his second stint with the club.
