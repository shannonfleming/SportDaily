**The NBA has proposed changes to its draft lottery system, which could significantly impact the Portland Trail Blazers and other teams.**

## Context and Background
The proposed '3-2-1' system aims to reduce tanking by changing the odds for the top picks. This new system would give the worst team a 14% chance of getting the first pick, with the next two worst teams having 13% and 12% chances, respectively.

## Tactical Key Points
The new system is designed to make it more difficult for teams to deliberately lose games in order to get a higher draft pick. Here are some key points about the proposed system:
* The worst team will have a 14% chance of getting the first pick
* The next two worst teams will have 13% and 12% chances, respectively
* The system is designed to reduce tanking and make the draft more competitive

> The NBA's new anti-tanking plan is expected to increase the number of trades, according to Mavericks co-owner Mark Cuban.

## What's Next?
The proposed changes are expected to be implemented in the upcoming season, and teams are already adjusting their strategies in response. The Trail Blazers, in particular, will need to adapt to the new system in order to remain competitive.
