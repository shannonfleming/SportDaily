**Sky Sports has secured a major broadcasting deal, extending its partnership with Formula 1 until 2034.**
## Context / Background
The new deal ensures that Sky Sports will remain the exclusive home of Formula 1 in the UK and Ireland for an additional five years. This extension solidifies Sky Sports' position as a leading broadcaster of premier sports events.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Category | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Broadcasting Rights | Exclusive to Sky Sports in the UK and Ireland |
| Duration | Extended until 2034 |
| Sport | Formula 1 |

> The extension of this partnership is a significant development for both Sky Sports and Formula 1, showcasing the strength of their relationship and commitment to delivering high-quality sports content.

## What's Next? (Schedule)
The exact schedule for the upcoming F1 season has not been fully announced, but with this deal in place, fans can expect uninterrupted and exclusive coverage on Sky Sports.
