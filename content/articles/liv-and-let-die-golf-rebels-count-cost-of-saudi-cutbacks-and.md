   **LIV Golf is facing significant financial uncertainty as Saudi Arabia considers cutting back its support for the golf rebels.** 
   ## Context / Background
   LIV Golf has been a major player in the golf world since its inception, backed by significant funding from Saudi Arabia's Public Investment Fund (PIF). However, recent reports suggest that the PIF may be on the verge of cutting its support for LIV Golf, leaving the organization facing an uncertain future.
