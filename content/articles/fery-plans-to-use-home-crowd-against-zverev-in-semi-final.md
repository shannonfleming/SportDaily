**Britain's Arthur Fery is set to face off against Germany's Alexander Zverev in the Wimbledon semi-final, with Fery planning to utilize the home crowd to his advantage.**

## Context / Background
The upcoming semi-final match between Arthur Fery and Alexander Zverev is highly anticipated, with Fery looking to leverage the support of the home crowd at Wimbledon.

## Tactical Key Points
Fery's strategy to use the crowd to his advantage could be a crucial factor in the match, as the energy of the home crowd can often propel players to perform at their best.

> Fery has stated that he will use the crowd to "my advantage" against Zverev, indicating his intention to feed off the energy of the home supporters.

## What's Next?
The semi-final match between Fery and Zverev is scheduled to take place on Friday, with the winner advancing to the final round of the tournament.
