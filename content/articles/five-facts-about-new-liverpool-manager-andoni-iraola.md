**Andoni Iraola has been appointed as the new manager of Liverpool, marking a significant change in the team's leadership.**
## Context / Background
Andoni Iraola's appointment comes after the sacking of Arne Slot, who failed to meet expectations. Iraola is known for his dynamic football style, which is expected to energize the team.
