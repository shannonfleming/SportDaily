**Liverpool is on the hunt for a new winger to replace the departing Mohamed Salah, with Yan Diomande and Bradley Barcola being considered as top contenders.**

## Context / Background
The recent speculation surrounding Mohamed Salah's future at Liverpool has sparked a heated debate among fans and pundits alike. With the Egyptian winger's contract set to expire, the Reds are scrambling to find a suitable replacement. Henry Winter and Sam Tighe, renowned football experts, recently discussed the possibility of Yan Diomande or Bradley Barcola joining Liverpool on the Transfer Talk Podcast.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
Both Diomande and Barcola have impressed in their respective leagues, showcasing their exceptional skills and potential to become world-class wingers. While Diomande's pace and agility make him an attractive option, Barcola's vision and passing range are undeniable assets.

## What the Experts Say
> Speaking on the Transfer Talk Podcast, Henry Winter and Sam Tighe discuss whether Yan Diomande or Bradley Barcola could join Liverpool this summer as a replacement for Mohamed Salah.

## What's Next?
As the summer transfer window approaches, Liverpool will need to make a decision quickly to avoid missing out on their top targets. With several other clubs also vying for the signatures of Diomande and Barcola, the Reds will need to act swiftly to secure their preferred choice.
