**The Cincinnati Bengals have made a significant move to strengthen their defense with a draft deal for Dexter Lawrence.** 
## Context / Background
The Bengals have been looking to improve their defensive lineup, and the acquisition of Dexter Lawrence is a major step in that direction. Lawrence, a talented defensive tackle, is expected to bring a new level of intensity and skill to the team's defense.

## Tactical Key Points
The deal involves the Bengals trading their No. 10 pick to the Giants in exchange for Lawrence. This move is seen as a strategic one, as it allows the Bengals to address a key need on their roster while also giving the Giants a valuable draft pick.
