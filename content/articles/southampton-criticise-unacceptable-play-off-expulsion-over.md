**Southampton's expulsion from the Championship play-off final has sparked controversy, with the club describing the punishment as 'manifestly disproportionate to the offence'.**

## Context / Background
The 'spygate' scandal has led to Southampton's expulsion from the Championship play-off final, a decision the club claims is unacceptable despite admitting to breaching EFL regulations.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The EFL regulations are in place to ensure fair play, but Southampton believes the punishment does not fit the crime. The club's chief executive has expressed disappointment and frustration with the decision.

> The punishment is unacceptable and we will be seeking further action to address this situation.

## What's Next?
Southampton will need to regroup and focus on their next steps, which may include an appeal against the decision. The club's fans will be eager to see how the situation unfolds and what the future holds for their team.
