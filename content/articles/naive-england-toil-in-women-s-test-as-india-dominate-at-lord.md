**England are facing a significant challenge to avoid defeat in the first Women's Test at Lord's after India dominated the second day of the match.**

## Context / Background
The first Women's Test between England and India is underway at Lord's, with the visitors taking control on the second day. England's performance has been described as 'naive', highlighting the team's struggles against India's dominant display.

## Tactical Key Points
India extended their lead to 269 runs, putting pressure on England's batting lineup. The hosts will need to regroup and strategize to avoid defeat in the four-day fixture.

> England's batting lineup will need to show resilience and determination to overcome the significant lead India has established.
