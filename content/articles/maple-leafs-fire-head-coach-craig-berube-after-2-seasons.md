**The Toronto Maple Leafs have made a significant change in their coaching staff by firing head coach Craig Berube after two seasons.**
## Context / Background
The decision comes after the Maple Leafs missed the playoffs for the first time since 2016. Berube's tenure was marked by high expectations but ultimately failed to deliver the desired results. The team's performance under his guidance was deemed unsatisfactory, leading to his dismissal.

## What's Next?
The Maple Leafs own the No. 1 pick in the 2026 NHL Draft, which could be a crucial step in rebuilding the team. The search for a new head coach will begin immediately, with several candidates expected to be considered for the position.
