**Rugby league legend John Kear has passed away at the age of 71, just a day after commentating on the Challenge Cup final.**
## Context / Background
John Kear was a renowned rugby league coach and broadcaster, best known for his work with the BBC. He had a long and distinguished career in the sport, both as a player and a coach.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
Kear's coaching career spanned several decades, with notable successes in the Challenge Cup and other competitions. His expertise and knowledge of the game made him a respected voice in the rugby league community.

> As a coach and broadcaster, John Kear was a true legend of the sport, and his passing will be deeply felt by all who knew him and followed his career.

## What's Next?
The rugby league community will come together to pay tribute to John Kear's memory and celebrate his contributions to the sport. Further details on memorial services and tributes will be announced in the coming days.
