**Conor McGregor has announced that he will undergo surgery after suffering an injury at UFC 329.** 
## Context / Background
Conor McGregor's highly anticipated fight at UFC 329 ended in disappointment as he suffered an injury on the opening kick. The exact nature of the injury has not been disclosed, but it is severe enough to require surgical intervention.

## What Happened
> The injury occurred when McGregor attempted a kick that did not connect as planned, resulting in an awkward landing that caused the injury.

## What's Next?
Conor McGregor has stated that he plans to fight again after recovering from his surgery. The timeline for his return to the UFC is currently unknown.
