## Context / Background
The boxing world is abuzz with the news of a rematch between two of the sport's all-time greats, Floyd Mayweather and Manny Pacquiao. The pair first faced off in 2015, with Mayweather emerging victorious via unanimous decision. Since then, both fighters have continued to compete at the highest level, with Pacquiao winning multiple world titles and Mayweather retiring and unretiring on multiple occasions.
