**Igor Tudor's tenure as Tottenham head coach has come to an abrupt end after just 44 days in charge.**

## Context / Background
Igor Tudor was appointed as the head coach of Tottenham Hotspur in a move that was seen as a surprise by many. Despite his experience in managing clubs like Marseille and Hajduk Split, his stint in the Premier League was short-lived. The Croatian manager failed to secure a single win in the Premier League during his time at the club.
