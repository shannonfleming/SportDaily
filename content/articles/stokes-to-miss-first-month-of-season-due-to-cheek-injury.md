    **England Test captain Ben Stokes is set to miss the first month of the County Championship season due to a cheek injury.**
    ## Context / Background
    The English cricket season is about to begin, with the County Championship being one of the most prestigious domestic competitions. Ben Stokes, the England Test captain, was expected to play a crucial role for his team, Durham.
    ## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
    Unfortunately, Stokes will not be able to participate in the initial stages of the season due to a gruesome cheek injury that required facial surgery. This is a significant blow to Durham's chances, as Stokes is one of the most experienced and skilled players in the team.
    > Although the exact details of the injury are not publicly known, it is clear that Stokes will need time to recover and regain his fitness before returning to the pitch.
    ## What's Next?
    The County Championship season is scheduled to continue without Stokes for the first month. Durham will have to rely on other players to fill the gap left by their captain's absence.
