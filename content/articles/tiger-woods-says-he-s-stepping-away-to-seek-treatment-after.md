**Tiger Woods has announced that he will be stepping away from golf to seek treatment after pleading not guilty to DUI charges.**

## Context / Background
Tiger Woods was involved in a rollover crash and was found to have two opioid pills in his pocket. He told police that he was looking at his phone before the crash. Woods has pleaded not guilty to the charges and has announced that he will be seeking treatment.
