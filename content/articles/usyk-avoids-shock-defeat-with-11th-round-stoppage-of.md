**Oleksandr Usyk has successfully defended his heavyweight championship title with an 11th-round stoppage of Rico Verhoeven.**
## Context / Background
Oleksandr Usyk, the reigning heavyweight champion, faced a significant challenge from Rico Verhoeven, a skilled kickboxer, in a highly anticipated match. The bout was expected to be closely contested, with both fighters having a strong chance of winning.

Usyk's experience and skill ultimately paid off, as he managed to overcome Verhoeven's impressive kickboxing skills and secure the stoppage in the 11th round.
> The stoppage victory marks a significant win for Usyk, who has solidified his position as the heavyweight champion.
