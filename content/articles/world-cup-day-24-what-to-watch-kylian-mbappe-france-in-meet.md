**The World Cup has reached its Round of 16 stage, with France and Morocco advancing to the quarterfinals after winning their respective matches.**

## Context / Background
The World Cup has been filled with exciting matches, and Day 24 was no exception. France, led by Kylian Mbappé, faced Paraguay, while Canada took on Morocco. The stakes were high, with the winners advancing to the quarterfinals.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
France's victory over Paraguay was a testament to their strength and depth. Morocco, on the other hand, dominated Canada, securing a convincing win. The results have set the stage for the quarterfinals, with fans eagerly anticipating the next round of matches.

> 'We were the better team,' said Canada's Jesse Marsch after their loss to Morocco, highlighting the competitive nature of the tournament.

## What's Next?
The World Cup will continue with the remaining Round of 16 matches, followed by the quarterfinals. Fans can expect more thrilling encounters as the tournament progresses.
