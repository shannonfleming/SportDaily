**Katherine Legge is set to make history by becoming the first woman to attempt the most daunting task in motorsports: completing the Indy 500 and Coca-Cola 600 on the same day.**

## Context / Background
Katherine Legge has announced her plans to participate in both the Indy 500 and the Coca-Cola 600, two of the most prestigious racing events in the world. This feat has never been achieved by a woman before, and Legge is determined to make history.

## What's Next? (Schedule)
The Indy 500 and Coca-Cola 600 are scheduled to take place on the same day, with the Indy 500 starting in the morning and the Coca-Cola 600 starting in the evening.
