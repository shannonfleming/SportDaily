## Context / Background
Ipswich Town, a professional football club based in Suffolk, England, has been at the center of controversy after Nigel Farage paid a surprise visit to the team's stadium, Portman Road. The visit was met with significant backlash from fans and the general public, with many expressing their discontent on social media.
