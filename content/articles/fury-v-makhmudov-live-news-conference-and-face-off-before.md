**Tyson Fury is set to return to the ring in April to face Arslanbek Makhmudov, and the news conference and face-off have been highly anticipated.**

## Context / Background
The boxing world has been abuzz with the news of Tyson Fury's return to the ring. The Briton has been out of action for some time but has announced his comeback, citing the death of Anthony Joshua's friends as a prompt for his return. Fury will face Arslanbek Makhmudov in April, and the two fighters have been engaging in a war of words in the lead-up to the fight.
