**Tottenham's managerial search is underway after the club sacked Thomas Frank, marking their fifth permanent manager in seven years. The question on everyone's mind is who will be the next to take the reins.**

## Context / Background
Tottenham's recent history has been marred by managerial changes, with each new appointment bringing a mix of hope and uncertainty. The club's inability to find stability has impacted their performance on the pitch, and the latest sacking of Thomas Frank has sparked a renewed search for the right leader.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The candidates to replace Frank include Roberto De Zerbi, known for his tactical prowess, Oliver Glasner, with his experience in the Bundesliga, and the potential return of Mauricio Pochettino, a fan favorite. Each candidate brings a unique set of skills and experiences that could shape Tottenham's future.

> The next manager will have to address the team's current form and work towards rebuilding a cohesive unit that can compete at the top level.

## What's Next?
