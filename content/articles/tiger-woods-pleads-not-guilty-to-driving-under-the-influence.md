**Tiger Woods has pleaded not guilty to a charge of driving under the influence.**
## Context / Background
Tiger Woods, the renowned American golfer, has been involved in a car crash that led to a DUI charge. The incident has sparked widespread media attention and public interest.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The details of the incident are still emerging, but it is reported that Woods had pills in his pocket and 'bloodshot' eyes after looking down at his phone before the car crash.
> I will do everything in my power to regain the trust of the people who have supported me, Woods said in a statement after the incident.
## What's Next?
Woods has stated that he will step away from golf and seek treatment after the DUI charge. The golfing world is waiting to see how this development will affect his career.
