**The Oakland Raiders have confirmed that they will not be trading Maxx Crosby, citing medical concerns as the primary reason.**

## Context / Background
The news comes after reports emerged that the Ravens had pulled out of a potential trade deal for Crosby due to concerns over his medical history. The Raiders have since moved to reassure fans that Crosby is an integral part of their team and will not be leaving.
