**Southampton Football Club has been expelled from the play-offs due to a spying scandal.** 
## Context / Background
The English Football League (EFL) has taken strict action against Southampton Football Club, expelling them from the play-offs. This decision comes after the club was found guilty of spying on their opponents, including Middlesbrough, Oxford, and Ipswich.

> The EFL has stated that Southampton's actions were a serious breach of regulations and have taken appropriate action to maintain the integrity of the competition.

## What's Next?
Southampton has lost their appeal against the expulsion, and the EFL has stood by their decision. The club's chief executive has described the punishment as 'manifestly disproportionate' to the offence.
