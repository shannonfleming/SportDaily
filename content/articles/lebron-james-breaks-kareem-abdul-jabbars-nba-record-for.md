**LeBron James has made history by breaking Kareem Abdul-Jabbar's record for the most career field goals in the NBA.**
## Context / Background
The record for the most career field goals in the NBA has been held by Kareem Abdul-Jabbar for many years. However, LeBron James has been steadily closing in on the record and has now surpassed Abdul-Jabbar to take the top spot.

| Player | Career Field Goals |
| --- | --- |
| LeBron James | 38,390 |
