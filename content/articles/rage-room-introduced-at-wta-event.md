    **The WTA has taken a unique approach to helping players manage their emotions during tournaments by introducing a 'rage room' at an event in Texas.**
    ## Context / Background
    The concept of a 'rage room' is not new, but its application in a tennis tournament setting is certainly innovative. This move by the WTA aims to provide players with a private space to express their frustrations away from the public eye.
    ## Tactical Key Points 
    | Feature | Description |
    | --- | --- |
    | Private Space | No cameras allowed |
    | Purpose | For players to manage stress and frustration |
    ## Key Insights
    > 'I like it, no cameras, it's private... it's a great idea' - This sentiment reflects the positive reception of the 'rage room' concept among players and commentators, including Tim Henman.
