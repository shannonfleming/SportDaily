**The ongoing case of Manchester City's 115 charges has been deemed 'damaging' for the Premier League by La Liga chief Javier Tebas.**

## Context / Background
Manchester City has been facing 115 charges related to Financial Fair Play (FFP) regulations. The case has been ongoing for three years, and a verdict is yet to be reached. The delay in the verdict has raised concerns among football officials and fans.

> 'The handling of the case is damaging for the Premier League,' said Javier Tebas, La Liga chief.

## What's Next?
The verdict for the case is expected to be announced soon. If Manchester City is found guilty, they could face a 60-point deduction, which would significantly impact their standings in the Premier League.
