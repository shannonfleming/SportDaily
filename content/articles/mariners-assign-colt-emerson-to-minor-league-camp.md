**The Seattle Mariners have made a significant decision regarding their top prospect, Colt Emerson, assigning him to minor league camp.**
## Context / Background
The Mariners' decision to demote Colt Emerson to minor league camp has ended his bid to make the opening day roster. Emerson, the team's number one prospect, will start the 2026 season in Triple-A.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Player | Assignment |
| --- | --- |
| Colt Emerson | Minor League Camp |

> The assignment of Colt Emerson to minor league camp is a strategic decision by the Mariners to allow him to develop further before potentially joining the major league team.

## What's Next?
The Mariners will continue to evaluate Colt Emerson's performance in minor league camp, and he may be considered for a call-up to the major league team later in the season.
