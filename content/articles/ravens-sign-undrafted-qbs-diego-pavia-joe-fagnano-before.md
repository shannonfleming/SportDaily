**The Baltimore Ravens have made moves to strengthen their quarterback depth by signing undrafted quarterbacks Diego Pavia and Joe Fagnano ahead of their rookie minicamp.**

## Context / Background
The Ravens' decision to sign Pavia and Fagnano comes as the team prepares for the upcoming season, looking to bolster their roster with young talent. Diego Pavia, a standout player from Vanderbilt, has been making headlines with his impressive performances, even being a Heisman finalist. Joe Fagnano also brings promise, having shown his skills in college football.
