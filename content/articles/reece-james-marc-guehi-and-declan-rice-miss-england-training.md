**England's World Cup campaign has been dealt a blow as key players miss training ahead of the quarterfinals.**

## Context / Background
England is set to face Norway in the quarterfinals of the World Cup, but their preparations have been disrupted by injuries to key players. Reece James, Marc Guéhi, and Declan Rice were all absent from training, sparking concerns about their availability for the crucial match.
