**Tiger Woods, the renowned American golfer, has been released on bail after being charged with driving under the influence (DUI) following a car crash in Florida.**
## Context and Background
Tiger Woods is widely regarded as one of the greatest golfers of all time, with a career spanning over two decades and numerous accolades, including 15 major championships. However, his personal life has been marred by controversies, including a highly publicized divorce and struggles with injuries.
## Key Points and Statistics
* Woods was arrested at the scene of the crash on suspicion of DUI
* He was later released on bail
* The incident has sparked widespread media attention and raised concerns about Woods' personal well-being
> 'Tiger Woods is a superhuman golfer who cannot escape his very human flaws'
## What's Next?
As the investigation into the crash continues, Woods is likely to face further scrutiny and potential disciplinary action. His fans and sponsors will be watching closely to see how he responds to this latest setback.
