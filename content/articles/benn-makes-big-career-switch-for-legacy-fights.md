## Context / Background
Conor Benn's decision to join Dana White and Zuffa Boxing marks a new chapter in his career. After parting ways with Eddie Hearn's Matchroom, Benn is looking to take his career to new heights.

## Tactical Key Points
Benn's move is seen as a strategic decision to gain access to higher-profile fights and increase his global recognition. With Zuffa Boxing, he will have the opportunity to compete against top-tier opponents and potentially become a household name.

> Benn wants 'legacy fights' and 'the biggest stages', indicating his ambition to leave a lasting impact on the sport.
