**Egypt has expressed strong criticism over the refereeing in their recent World Cup match against Argentina, citing influential decisions that they believe affected the outcome of the game.**

## Context / Background
The Egyptian Football Association has publicly stated their dissatisfaction with the officiating in the match, suggesting that certain decisions were unfair and had a significant impact on the result. This comes amid a series of controversies surrounding refereeing in the World Cup.
