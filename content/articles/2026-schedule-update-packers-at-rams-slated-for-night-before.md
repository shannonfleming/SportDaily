**The NFL has announced a historic matchup between the Green Bay Packers and the Los Angeles Rams, scheduled to take place on the night before Thanksgiving in 2026.** 
## Context / Background
The NFL has been exploring ways to expand its reach and create new traditions. This game marks the first time an NFL matchup will be played on Thanksgiving Eve, potentially paving the way for future games on this day.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Team | Previous Thanksgiving Appearances |
| --- | --- |
| Green Bay Packers | 36 |
| Los Angeles Rams | 4 |

> The Packers have a rich history of playing on Thanksgiving Day, but this will be their first time playing on the eve of the holiday.

## What's Next?
The game is part of a five-game package that will be streamed on Netflix, marking a significant partnership between the NFL and the streaming giant. The full NFL schedule for 2026 is expected to be released soon, with more games and matchups to be announced.
