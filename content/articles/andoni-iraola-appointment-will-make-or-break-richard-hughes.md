**The appointment of Andoni Iraola as Liverpool's new manager could be a make-or-break moment for Richard Hughes, the club's sporting director.**

## Context / Background
Iraola's appointment comes after the sacking of Arne Slot, who wrote an emotional open letter to Liverpool supporters after his departure. The Dutch coach did not have the answers to Liverpool's problems, and the club's hierarchy has taken a risk by appointing Iraola.
