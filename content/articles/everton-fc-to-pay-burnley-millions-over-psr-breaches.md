**Executive Summary**: Everton FC has been ordered to pay Burnley millions of pounds in compensation over Premier League relegation legal case due to breaches of the Premier League's Profit and Sustainability Regulations (PSR).

## Context / Background
The decision comes after a lengthy legal battle between the two clubs, with Everton appealing the initial order to pay £40m in compensation. The case revolves around Everton's breaches of the Premier League's PSR, which governs the financial sustainability of clubs in the league.
