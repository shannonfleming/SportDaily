**Tyson Fury is set to make his highly anticipated return to the boxing ring, with his sights firmly set on a rematch against Oleksandr Usyk.**

## Context / Background
Tyson Fury's promoter, Frank Warren, has revealed that Fury is eager to face his old rival Usyk once again. This comes as Fury prepares for his comeback fight, which is expected to be a major stepping stone in his bid to reclaim his position as the best boxer in the world.

## Tactical Key Points
Fury's decision to target Usyk is a clear indication of his desire to be the best, and to prove his superiority over one of the toughest opponents in the division. With both fighters having a rich history of competition, this potential rematch is likely to be one of the most highly anticipated bouts of the year.

> Tyson Fury wants to fight Oleksandr Usyk again. He wants to be the best, says promoter Frank Warren.
