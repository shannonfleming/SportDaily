**The stage is set for the 2026 NBA Finals as the New York Knicks prepare to face off against the San Antonio Spurs.**

## Context / Background
The Knicks and Spurs have both had impressive playoff runs, with the Spurs advancing to the NBA Finals after a thrilling 111-103 win over the Thunder. The Knicks, on the other hand, have been dominant in the Eastern Conference, with a strong team effort that has carried them to the Finals.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The Spurs will be looking to their star players to lead the charge, while the Knicks will be relying on their strong team defense to shut down the Spurs' offense.

> The Spurs' ability to adapt to the Knicks' defense will be crucial in determining the outcome of the series.

## What's Next? (Schedule)
The NBA Finals are set to begin, with the first game scheduled to take place at Madison Square Garden. The series is expected to be a closely contested one, with both teams having a strong chance of winning.
