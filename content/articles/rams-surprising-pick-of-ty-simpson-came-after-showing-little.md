**The Los Angeles Rams have made a surprising move in the first round of the NFL draft, selecting Alabama quarterback Ty Simpson.** 
## Context / Background
The Rams showed little interest in Simpson before the draft, making the pick a shock to many. Simpson, who played for Alabama, was considered a top prospect but not necessarily a first-round pick by many experts.

> The Rams' decision to draft Simpson has been met with mixed reactions, with some praising the team's willingness to take a chance on a talented young player and others questioning the move.

## What's Next?
The Rams will look to integrate Simpson into their roster and see how he develops as a player. With the NFL season still months away, it will be interesting to see how Simpson performs in training camp and preseason games.
