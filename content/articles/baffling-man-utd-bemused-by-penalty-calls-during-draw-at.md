**Manchester United's 2-2 draw with Bournemouth has left the team and its manager, Michael Carrick, bewildered by the penalty calls made during the match.** 
## Context / Background
The match saw Harry Maguire sent off and giving away a penalty, while Bournemouth's Adrien Truffert escaped punishment for a similar challenge on Amad Diallo. This inconsistency has raised questions about the fairness of the refereeing decisions.
