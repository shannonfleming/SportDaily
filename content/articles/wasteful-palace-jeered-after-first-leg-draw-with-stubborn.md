Crystal Palace hosted Larnaca in the first leg of their Europa League last-16 tie, with the home team seeking to assert their dominance. However, Palace's labored performance was marked by missed opportunities, including fine chances squandered by Evann Guessand, Tyrick Mitchell, and Chris Richards.
