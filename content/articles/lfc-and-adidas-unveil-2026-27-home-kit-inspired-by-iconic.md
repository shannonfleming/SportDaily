**Liverpool FC and adidas have unveiled the new home kit for the 2026-27 season, drawing inspiration from the club's iconic title-winning campaigns.**
## Context / Background
The new kit is a tribute to the club's rich history and is expected to be a hit among fans. The design features a classic look with a modern twist, incorporating the latest technology from adidas.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The kit features a bold red color scheme, with white accents and a unique pattern on the sleeves. The shirt also includes a special badge commemorating the club's title-winning seasons.
> The new kit is a great way to connect with our history and heritage, while also looking to the future.
## What's Next?
The kit will be available for purchase starting next week, with fans able to get their hands on the new design ahead of the upcoming season.
