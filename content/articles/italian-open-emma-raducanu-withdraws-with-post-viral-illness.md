**Emma Raducanu's Italian Open campaign has come to an abrupt end due to a post-viral illness.** 
## Context / Background
The British tennis star was set to compete in the Italian Open but withdrew shortly after a media conference. Raducanu had been looking forward to her first match since March and was in line to face Coco Gauff in the Rome draw.

## What's Next?
The Italian Open will continue without Raducanu, with other top players vying for the title. Fans will have to wait and see when Raducanu will return to the court.
