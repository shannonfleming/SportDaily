**Alexander Volkanovski has issued a warning to Movsar Evloev ahead of their potential fight, advising him not to use the same strategy he employed against Lerone Murphy.**

## Context / Background
Alexander Volkanovski, the current UFC Featherweight Champion, has been vocal about his desire to face Movsar Evloev, who recently stunned Lerone Murphy at UFC London. Volkanovski believes that Evloev's performance against Murphy showcased his skills, but also highlighted a potential weakness that he could exploit.

## What's Next?
The UFC has not officially announced a fight between Volkanovski and Evloev, but the two fighters are expected to meet in the near future. Volkanovski has been dominant in his title reign, and a fight against Evloev would be a tough test for the Russian fighter.
