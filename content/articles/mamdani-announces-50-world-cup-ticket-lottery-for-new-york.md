**Executive Summary**: New York City Mayor Zohran Mamdani has announced a $50 World Cup ticket lottery for New York City residents, making the tournament more accessible to the general public.

## Context / Background
The World Cup is one of the most widely viewed and followed sporting events globally. However, the high cost of tickets often makes it difficult for average fans to attend. In an effort to make the event more inclusive, Mayor Mamdani has secured 1,000 affordable tickets for New Yorkers.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The ticket lottery is part of a broader initiative to increase accessibility and affordability for sports fans in New York City. Key points of the initiative include:
* 1,000 tickets available at $50 each
* Tickets will be distributed through a lottery system
* The initiative aims to make sports more accessible to the general public

> 'We don’t want sports to become a luxury commodity' - Zohran Mamdani

## What's Next?
The ticket lottery is set to take place in the coming weeks, with exact dates to be announced. Fans interested in participating can stay tuned for further updates on the application process and timeline.
