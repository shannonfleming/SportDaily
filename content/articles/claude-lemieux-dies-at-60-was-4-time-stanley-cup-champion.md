**Claude Lemieux, a four-time Stanley Cup champion, has passed away at the age of 60.**
## Context / Background
Claude Lemieux was a renowned Canadian professional ice hockey player who played in the National Hockey League (NHL) for several teams, including the Montreal Canadiens, New Jersey Devils, Colorado Avalanche, and Dallas Stars. He won the Stanley Cup four times during his career.
