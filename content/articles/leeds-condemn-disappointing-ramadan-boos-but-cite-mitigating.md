**Leeds United has officially condemned the booing of players breaking their Ramadan fast during their match against Manchester City, citing it as 'unexpected and disappointing' while also outlining mitigating circumstances.**

## Context / Background
The incident occurred during a scheduled pause in the game to allow Muslim players to break their fast. The reaction from the crowd was widely criticized, prompting Leeds to issue a statement addressing the situation.

| Manchester City | Respectful pause | Maintained their lead, securing a win |

> The club's statement highlighted the 'mitigating circumstances' that may have contributed to the crowd's reaction, emphasizing the need for understanding and respect for all players, regardless of their faith or background.

## What's Next?
Leeds will look to move past this incident, focusing on their upcoming matches and improving their performance in the Premier League. The team's response to the situation will be closely watched, as they aim to regain the support and respect of their fans.
