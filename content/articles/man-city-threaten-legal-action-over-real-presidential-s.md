## Context / Background
The situation has sparked a potential transfer dispute, with Manchester City taking a strong stance against the public declaration of intent to sign their player. The candidate's pledge has been seen as a direct attempt to lure Haaland away from Manchester City, which could potentially disrupt the player's current contract and commitment to the club.
