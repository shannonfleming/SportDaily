**Anthony Gordon is on the verge of a move to Barcelona, with the Newcastle winger set to undergo a medical ahead of a £69m transfer.**

## Context / Background
The 22-year-old has been a key player for Newcastle this season, and his impressive performances have caught the attention of several top European clubs. Barcelona have been leading the chase for Gordon's signature, and it appears that they have finally agreed a deal with Newcastle.

Gordon's move to Barcelona will see him more than double his current wages, with reports suggesting that he will earn around £300k per week at the Nou Camp.

## What's Next?
Gordon's transfer to Barcelona is expected to be completed in the coming days, with the winger set to undergo a medical in the next 48 hours. Once the deal is finalized, Gordon will become one of the most expensive signings in Barcelona's history.
