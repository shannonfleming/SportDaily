**The San Antonio Spurs have taken the lead in the NBA Western Conference Finals, defeating the Oklahoma City Thunder in Game 1.**

## Context / Background
The Spurs and Thunder faced off in Game 1 of the Western Conference Finals on Monday, May 18. The Thunder were on a playoff win streak, but the Spurs were determined to snap it.

> The Spurs' Victory Wembanyama had an incredible Game 1 performance, scoring 41 points and leading his team to a double-overtime thriller win.

## What's Next?
The Spurs and Thunder will face off again in Game 2 of the Western Conference Finals. The schedule for the series has not been fully announced, but fans can expect the next game to take place later this week.
