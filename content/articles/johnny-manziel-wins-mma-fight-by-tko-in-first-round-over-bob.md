**Johnny Manziel has secured a victory in his MMA debut, defeating social media influencer Bob Menery by TKO in the first round.**

## Context / Background
The fight, which was highly anticipated, took place at the Brand Risk 14 event. Johnny Manziel, a former NFL quarterback, was making his MMA debut against Bob Menery, a social media influencer.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Fighter | Result | Method | Round |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Johnny Manziel | Win | TKO | 1 |
| Bob Menery | Loss | TKO | 1 |

> 'I won't fight again,' Johnny Manziel said after the fight, indicating that this might be his only MMA appearance.

## What's Next?
Johnny Manziel has stated that he won't be continuing his MMA career, so this might be the last time we see him in the octagon. For now, fans can relish in his debut victory.
