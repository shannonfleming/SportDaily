**Team USA is on a roll in the World Baseball Classic, with their latest win coming against Great Britain.**
## Context / Background
The World Baseball Classic is an international baseball tournament that features teams from around the world. Team USA has been one of the top contenders in the tournament, with a strong lineup and talented pitching staff.
