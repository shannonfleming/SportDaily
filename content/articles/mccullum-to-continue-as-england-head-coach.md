**Brendon McCullum will retain his position as the head coach of the England cricket team, as confirmed by the England and Wales Cricket Board (ECB) following the Ashes review.**

## Context / Background
The decision to keep McCullum on board comes after a thorough review of England's performance in the Ashes series. Despite the crushing loss, the ECB has shown faith in McCullum's leadership and coaching abilities.

## Tactical Key Points
McCullum's coaching philosophy has been focused on aggressive and attacking cricket. The team's performance in the series was marked by some promising individual displays, but ultimately, they were unable to capitalize on these performances to win the series.

## Key Quote
> The ECB's decision to retain McCullum is a testament to their commitment to his vision for the team.

## What's Next?
With the Ashes review now complete, England will turn their attention to the upcoming World Cup. The team will be looking to regroup and refocus ahead of the tournament.
