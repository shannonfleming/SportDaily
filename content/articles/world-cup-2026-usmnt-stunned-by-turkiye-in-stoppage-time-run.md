**The USMNT's perfect group stage run has come to an end after a stunning stoppage-time loss to Türkiye.**

## Context / Background
The USMNT had been on a roll in the group stage, winning their first two matches and looking strong. However, their third match against Türkiye proved to be a different story. The Turks came out strong and were able to capitalize on a late mistake by the USMNT, scoring the winning goal in stoppage time.
