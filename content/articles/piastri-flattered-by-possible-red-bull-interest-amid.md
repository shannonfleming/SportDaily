**Oscar Piastri has expressed his surprise and flattery at being considered Red Bull's top target should Max Verstappen leave the team.**

## Context / Background
The Formula 1 season has been filled with speculation about Max Verstappen's future with Red Bull. Amidst the uncertainty, reports have emerged that Oscar Piastri, the McLaren driver, is Red Bull's preferred choice to replace Verstappen if he decides to leave.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
While there are no concrete stats available, it's clear that Piastri's performance has caught the attention of Red Bull. The young driver has shown immense potential, and his skills behind the wheel make him an attractive candidate for any top-tier team.

> I was flattered to hear that, but it came as news to me. I'm just focusing on my job with McLaren and trying to do the best I can.
