**The DR Congo national team is on the verge of making history as they prepare for their first World Cup appearance in over half a century, with defender Axel Tuanzebe expressing his hopes that football can bring about positive change beyond the pitch.**

## Context and Background
The Leopards are set to participate in the World Cup for the first time since 1974, a significant milestone for the country. However, their preparations are taking place against the backdrop of ongoing conflict back home, making their World Cup journey even more poignant.

## Tactical Key Points
As the team gears up for the tournament, they will be looking to make a lasting impact, both on and off the field. With Tuanzebe at the helm, the Leopards will be hoping to use their platform to raise awareness about the situation in their home country and promote unity through football.

> We're not politicians, but we can change the world with football, and that's what we're trying to do.

## What's Next?
The World Cup is set to kick off soon, with the Leopards facing tough competition in their group. As they take to the field, they will be carrying the hopes of their nation on their shoulders, and Tuanzebe's words will be a rallying cry for the team.
