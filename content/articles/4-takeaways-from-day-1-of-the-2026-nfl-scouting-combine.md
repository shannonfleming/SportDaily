## Context / Background
The NFL Scouting Combine is an annual event where college football players perform physical and mental tests in front of NFL coaches, general managers, and scouts. The combine is a crucial step in the NFL draft process, as it allows teams to evaluate players' skills and potential.

Some of the key takeaways from day 1 of the combine include:
* The performance of top quarterback prospects, including Garrett Nussmeier and CJ Allen
