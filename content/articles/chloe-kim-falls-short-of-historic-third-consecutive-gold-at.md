**Chloe Kim's bid for a historic third consecutive gold medal in the halfpipe event at the Winter Olympics has ended in disappointment.**
## Context / Background
The Winter Olympics have been a stage for Chloe Kim to showcase her exceptional skills in the halfpipe event, having won two consecutive gold medals in the past. However, this time around, she faced stiff competition from other talented athletes.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
Chloe Kim's performance was not enough to secure the top spot on the podium, as she was outperformed by Gaon Choi. The final standings saw Kim taking the silver medal, falling short of her goal to make history with a third consecutive gold.
> Chloe Kim's silver medal performance is still an achievement to be proud of, but it's clear that she had her sights set on gold.
