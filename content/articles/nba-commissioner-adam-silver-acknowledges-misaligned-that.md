**NBA Commissioner Adam Silver has acknowledged the existence of 'misaligned incentives' that contribute to tanking in the league.**
## Context and Background
The issue of tanking has been a longstanding concern in the NBA, with several teams accused of intentionally losing games to secure better draft picks. Commissioner Adam Silver has been under pressure to address the issue and find a solution to prevent tanking.
## Tactical Key Points and Stats
| Team | Number of Tanking Allegations |
| --- | --- |
| Jazz | 2 |
| Pacers | 1 |
Silver has considered various remedies, including changing the draft structure and taking away picks from teams found guilty of tanking.
> The NBA has fined the Jazz $500,000 and the Pacers $100,000 for violating the league's player participation policy, a move seen as a warning to teams engaging in tanking practices.
## What's Next?
The NBA will continue to monitor the situation and work towards finding a solution to prevent tanking. Silver has stated that he is open to making changes to the draft structure and has not ruled out taking away picks from teams found guilty of tanking.
