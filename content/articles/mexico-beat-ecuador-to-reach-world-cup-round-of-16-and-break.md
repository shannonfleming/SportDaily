**Mexico has broken a 40-year curse by defeating Ecuador 2-0 to reach the World Cup Round of 16.**

## Context / Background
The match between Mexico and Ecuador was highly anticipated, with both teams looking to secure a spot in the next round of the World Cup. Mexico's victory marks their first knockout win in 40 years, a significant milestone for the team.
