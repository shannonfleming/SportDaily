**Eloy Room's Historic Performance Helps Curacao Secure a Point Against Ecuador.**

## Context / Background
The World Cup has seen its fair share of incredible goalkeeping performances, but Eloy Room's display against Ecuador will be remembered for a long time. The Curacao goalkeeper made 15 saves, tying the record for most saves in a single World Cup match, to help his team secure a 0-0 draw against a strong Ecuadorian side.
