**BBC has decided to scrap Football Focus after 52 years, a move that has shocked the football community.**
## Context / Background
The BBC has been broadcasting Football Focus for over five decades, providing fans with analysis, news, and interviews. The show has been a staple of British football culture, with many fans growing up watching the program.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The decision to scrap Football Focus is likely due to changing viewing habits and the rise of online content. The BBC has not released an official statement on the reasoning behind the decision, but it is expected that the corporation will focus on digital content in the future.
> The BBC has not commented on the future of the show's presenters and staff.
## What's Next?
The scrapping of Football Focus has left a void in the football broadcasting landscape. It is unclear what the BBC will replace the show with, but it is expected that the corporation will announce new programming in the coming weeks.
