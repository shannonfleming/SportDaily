   **The New York Jets have made the decision to place a $14 million franchise tag on star running back Breece Hall, as reported by multiple sources.**
   
   ## Context / Background
   The Jets had until the deadline to make a decision on whether to use the franchise tag on Hall, who has been a key player for the team. The franchise tag allows the Jets to retain Hall's services for the upcoming season while they work on a long-term contract extension.
   
   ## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
   | Category | Value |
   | --- | --- |
   | Franchise Tag Value | $14 million |
   | Player | Breece Hall |
   | Team | New York Jets |
   | Position | Running Back |
   
   > The Jets' decision to place the franchise tag on Hall is a clear indication of their commitment to keeping him on the team. However, it remains to be seen how Hall will react to the move and whether it will impact contract talks.
   
   ## What's Next?
   The Jets will now work on negotiating a long-term contract extension with Hall, who is considered one of the top running backs in the NFL. The team will need to balance their desire to keep Hall with the need to manage their salary cap.
