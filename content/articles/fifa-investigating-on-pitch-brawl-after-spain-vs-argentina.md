    **FIFA has launched an investigation into the on-pitch brawl that occurred after the Spain vs Argentina World Cup final.**
    ## Context / Background
    The World Cup final between Spain and Argentina ended in controversy as a brawl broke out on the pitch after the final whistle. The incident involved players and staff from both teams and has sparked widespread criticism.
    ## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
    | Team | Players Involved | Actions Taken |
    | --- | --- | --- |
    | Argentina | Leandro Paredes | Sparked brawl at final whistle |
    | Spain | Multiple players | Involved in altercation |
    ## Key Quote
    > 'The behavior of the Argentina team was unacceptable and has no place in the beautiful game.' - Joe Hart, Pundit
    ## What's Next?
    FIFA will conduct a thorough investigation into the incident and may impose disciplinary actions on the teams and players involved. The investigation is expected to be completed in the coming weeks.
