**Floyd Mayweather, a boxing legend, has announced his decision to come out of retirement, aiming to return to professional boxing after his exhibition fight with Mike Tyson in April.**

## Context / Background
Floyd Mayweather, who will turn 49 later this month, has been retired from professional boxing but has participated in various exhibition fights. His upcoming fight with Mike Tyson in April is highly anticipated, and now, with the announcement of his return to professional boxing, fans are eager to see what the future holds for the boxing legend.
