**The Los Angeles Rams have made a significant move in the NFL offseason by acquiring defensive end Myles Garrett from the Cleveland Browns in exchange for Jared Verse and draft picks.**

## Context / Background
The NFL offseason has been filled with trades and signings, and this move is one of the most notable. Myles Garrett is a talented defensive end who has been a key player for the Browns, while Jared Verse is a promising young player.
