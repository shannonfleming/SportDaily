**Bryson DeChambeau's Masters campaign came to an early end after a disappointing performance.**

## Context / Background
The Masters 2026 has seen its share of surprises, with Bryson DeChambeau being one of the notable casualties. DeChambeau, known for his unique approach to golf, including the use of a 3D-printed iron, failed to make the cut after a 6-over performance.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Player | Score | Position |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Bryson DeChambeau | 6-over | Missed Cut |

DeChambeau's performance was marred by a series of errors, including a tee shot that hit a patron and a bunker blowup at Amen Corner.

> Bryson DeChambeau's early exit from the Masters is a significant upset, given his reputation as one of the most innovative players in the game.

## What's Next?
The Masters 2026 will continue without DeChambeau, with other players vying for the top spot. Rory McIlroy is currently sharing the lead after an impressive start.
