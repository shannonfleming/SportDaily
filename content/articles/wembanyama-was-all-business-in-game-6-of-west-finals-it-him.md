**Victor Wembanyama's dominant performance in Game 6 has earned the San Antonio Spurs a spot in Game 7 of the Western Conference finals.**

## Context / Background
The San Antonio Spurs were facing elimination in Game 6 of the Western Conference finals against the Oklahoma City Thunder. However, Victor Wembanyama's impressive display ensured the Spurs would live to fight another day.
