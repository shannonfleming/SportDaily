**McLaren's team principal, Andrea Stella, has expressed his dissatisfaction with the lack of engine information provided by Mercedes, their engine supplier, following the Australian Grand Prix.**

## Context / Background
The relationship between engine suppliers and their customer teams in Formula 1 is crucial for performance and strategy. Mercedes, as one of the top engine suppliers, has a significant impact on the competitive edge of teams like McLaren. However, the recent Australian Grand Prix highlighted a significant gap in performance, prompting concerns about the flow of information.

> The lack of detailed information from Mercedes about their engine performance and strategies has hindered McLaren's ability to optimize their car's setup and race strategy.

## What's Next?
As the Formula 1 season progresses, the need for closer collaboration and information sharing between Mercedes and McLaren becomes more pressing. The upcoming races will be crucial in determining whether the issues can be addressed, potentially altering the competitive landscape of the season.
