**England's World Cup campaign came to an end with a defeat to Argentina, sparking questions over Thomas Tuchel's tactics.**
## Context / Background
England's World Cup exit has been met with disappointment and frustration, with many pointing to Tuchel's decision to switch to a more defensive approach as a key factor in the defeat. Argentina, on the other hand, demonstrated their resilience and determination, with Lionel Messi once again proving to be the difference-maker.
