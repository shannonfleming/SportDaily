    **Kimi Antonelli has made history by becoming the youngest F1 championship leader after winning the Japanese Grand Prix at Suzuka.**
    
    ## Context / Background
    The 2026 Japanese Grand Prix took place at the iconic Suzuka circuit, with Kimi Antonelli starting from pole position. The young driver faced stiff competition from his teammate George Russell and Oscar Piastri, but managed to hold on to the lead and take the chequered flag.
