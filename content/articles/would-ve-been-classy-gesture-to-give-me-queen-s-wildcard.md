**Dan Evans has expressed his disappointment and criticism towards the Lawn Tennis Association (LTA) for not offering him a wildcard for the HSBC Championships at Queen's Club.**
## Context / Background
The British tennis star is nearing retirement and felt that receiving a wildcard for the prestigious tournament would have been a fitting gesture. Evans believes that the LTA lacked class in their decision-making process.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The LTA's decision has sparked debate among tennis fans and experts, with some arguing that Evans deserves the wildcard based on his contributions to British tennis.
## Key Quote
> 'Would've been classy gesture to give me Queen's wildcard' - Dan Evans
## What's Next?
The HSBC Championships at Queen's Club are set to take place this week, and fans will be watching to see how the tournament unfolds without Evans' participation.
