**Michigan Athletic Director Warde Manuel has announced his decision to step down by the end of the calendar year, following an internal investigation into the university's athletic department.**

## Context / Background
The decision comes after an internal investigation found 'barriers' and other issues within the athletic department. The investigation has prompted changes and reforms within the department.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Issue | Finding |
| --- | --- |
| Barriers | Identified within the athletic department |
| Reforms | Changes promised by the university |

> 'Forever, Go Blue': University of Michigan Athletic Director Warde Manuel issued a statement on his departure, marking the end of an era for the athletic department.

## What's Next?
The university will begin the process of finding a new athletic director, with the goal of having a replacement in place by the start of the new year. The athletic department will continue to implement reforms and changes in response to the internal investigation.
