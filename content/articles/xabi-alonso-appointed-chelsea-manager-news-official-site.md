**Xabi Alonso has been appointed as the new manager of Chelsea Football Club, marking a significant change in the team's leadership.**

## Context / Background
Xabi Alonso's appointment comes after a thorough search for a new manager, following the departure of the previous manager. Alonso, a former player and coach, brings a wealth of experience to the role, having played for several top-tier clubs and coached in the Bundesliga.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
Alonso's coaching style is expected to focus on possession-based football, with an emphasis on developing young players and creating a strong team dynamic. His previous coaching experience has shown promising results, with a win rate of over 50% in his last season.

> The appointment of Xabi Alonso is a significant moment for Chelsea, and fans will be eager to see how he shapes the team's future.

## What's Next?
Alonso's first match in charge is expected to be a crucial test of his managerial skills, as Chelsea looks to bounce back from a disappointing FA Cup final defeat.
