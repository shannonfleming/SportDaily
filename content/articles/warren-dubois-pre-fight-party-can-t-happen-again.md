**Executive Summary**: Frank Warren, the promoter of Daniel Dubois, has expressed his disappointment and concern over the pre-fight party that took place before Dubois' defeat to Oleksandr Usyk, stating that such an event 'can't happen again'.

## Context / Background
The pre-fight party has been cited as a potential factor in Dubois' loss, with many believing it may have affected his preparation and focus for the big match. Frank Warren has come out to address the issue, emphasizing the importance of a disciplined approach to training and match preparation.
