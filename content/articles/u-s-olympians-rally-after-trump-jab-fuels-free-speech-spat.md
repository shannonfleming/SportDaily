**US Olympians have come together to condemn recent comments made by former President Donald Trump, with many calling his words 'pretty childish'.**

## Context / Background
The controversy began when Trump made a comment about an Olympian being a 'loser', sparking widespread criticism from the athletic community. Many US Olympians have since spoken out against the comment, advocating for tolerance and respect.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Olympian | Response to Trump's Comment |
| --- | --- |
| Chloe Kim | Spoke out against Trump's comment, calling it 'unacceptable' |
| Eileen Gu | Advocated for free speech and respect for all athletes |

> 'It's pretty childish to make comments like that, especially about someone who is representing their country' - US Olympian

## What's Next?
The US Olympic team will continue to compete in the Winter Olympics, with many athletes using their platform to speak out against hate and promote tolerance.
