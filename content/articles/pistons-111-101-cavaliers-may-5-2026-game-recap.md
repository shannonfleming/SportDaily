**The Detroit Pistons have taken the lead in their series against the Cleveland Cavaliers with a 111-101 win in Game 1.**
## Context / Background
The Pistons, looking to overcome their postseason struggles against the Cavaliers, came out strong in this first game of the Eastern Conference semis. Their last win against the Cavaliers in the postseason was a long time coming, and this victory marks a significant turnaround.
