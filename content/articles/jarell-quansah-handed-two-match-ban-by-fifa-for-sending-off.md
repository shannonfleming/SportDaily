**Jarell Quansah has been handed a two-match ban by Fifa following his sending off against Mexico.**
## Context / Background
Jarell Quansah's recent match against Mexico ended in a red card for the player, leading to a Fifa review of the incident. Following the review, Quansah has been handed a two-match ban.
