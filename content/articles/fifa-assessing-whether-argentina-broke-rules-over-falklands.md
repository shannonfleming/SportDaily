**Fifa has launched an investigation into Argentina's potential breach of rules after the team displayed a Falklands banner.**
## Context / Background
The incident occurred during a recent match, sparking controversy and debate. The Falklands banner has been a point of contention between Argentina and the UK.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Team | Incident | Potential Outcome |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Argentina | Displayed Falklands banner | Rule breach investigation |

> The incident has drawn attention from various parties, including the UK government, which has reaffirmed its claim over the Falkland Islands.

## What's Next?
Fifa will continue to assess the situation and determine whether Argentina has breached any rules. The outcome of the investigation may have implications for the team's participation in the World Cup.
