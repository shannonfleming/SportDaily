**The Indianapolis Colts have given quarterback Anthony Richardson permission to seek a trade, according to recent reports.** 
## Context / Background
The Colts' decision to allow Richardson to seek a trade comes after a disappointing tenure with the team. Richardson was a top-five pick in the 2023 NFL draft but has struggled to find his footing in the league.
