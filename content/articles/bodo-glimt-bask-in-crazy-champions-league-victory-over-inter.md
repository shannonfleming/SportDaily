**Bodo/Glimt have pulled off one of the most significant upsets in recent Champions League history, defeating Inter Milan 2-1.**
## Context / Background
The Norwegian side, considered huge underdogs going into the match, defied all odds to secure a stunning victory over the Italian giants. This win marks a historic moment for Bodo/Glimt, a team from a small town in Norway, as they advance to the last 16 of the Champions League.

## What's Next?
Bodo/Glimt will now look forward to the draw for the last 16, eager to see who their next opponents will be. Their victory, along with those of Atleti, Leverkusen, and Newcastle, sets up an exciting phase of the competition.
