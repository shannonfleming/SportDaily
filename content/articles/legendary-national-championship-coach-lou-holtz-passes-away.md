**Legendary national championship coach Lou Holtz has passed away at the age of 89.**
## Context / Background
Lou Holtz was a renowned American football coach, best known for his tenure as the head coach of the Notre Dame Fighting Irish. During his time at Notre Dame, Holtz led the team to a national championship in 1988. His legacy extends beyond his coaching accomplishments, as he is widely regarded as one of the greatest coaches in college football history.
