**Conor McGregor is set to make his return to the UFC after a 5-year hiatus, facing off against Max Holloway at UFC 329. The weigh-ins have confirmed both fighters are on point for the highly anticipated rematch.**

## Context / Background
Conor McGregor's return to the UFC has been highly publicized, with many fans eagerly awaiting his first fight in 5 years. Max Holloway, on the other hand, is set to make his debut at 170 pounds, looking to take down the returning McGregor.
