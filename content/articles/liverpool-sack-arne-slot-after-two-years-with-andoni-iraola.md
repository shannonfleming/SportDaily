**Liverpool has parted ways with head coach Arne Slot after two years at the helm, with Andoni Iraola emerging as the top candidate to succeed him.**
## Context / Background
The decision to sack Arne Slot comes as a surprise to many, given the Dutchman's initial success at the club. However, a decline in form and performances over the past year led to increased pressure on the coach.
