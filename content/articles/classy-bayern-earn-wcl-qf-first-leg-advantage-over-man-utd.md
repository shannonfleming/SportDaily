    ## Context / Background
    The quarter-final tie between Bayern Munich and Manchester United was highly anticipated, with both teams looking to make a statement in the competition. Bayern Munich, with their strong squad and experienced players, were favorites to win, but Manchester United were determined to cause an upset.
