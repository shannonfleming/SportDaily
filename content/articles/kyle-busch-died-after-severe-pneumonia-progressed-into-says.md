**Kyle Busch, a two-time NASCAR Cup Series champion, has passed away at the age of 41 due to complications from severe pneumonia that progressed into sepsis, according to his family.**

## Context / Background
Kyle Busch was a renowned American professional stock car racing driver and team owner. He competed in the NASCAR Cup Series, driving the No. 8 Chevrolet for Richard Childress Racing. Busch was known for his exceptional driving skills and had won numerous awards throughout his career.
