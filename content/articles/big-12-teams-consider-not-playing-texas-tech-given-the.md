**The Big 12 conference is facing a potential crisis as teams consider not playing Texas Tech following the Brendan Sorsby ruling.**

## Context / Background
The situation arises after Texas Tech quarterback Brendan Sorsby was granted a temporary injunction to play in 2026 despite admitting to gambling. This decision has sparked controversy and raised questions about the integrity of college athletics.

## Reaction to the Ruling
> 'College athletics integrity left the building' after the judge's decision to let Sorsby play, according to critics of the ruling.

## What's Next?
The Big 12 teams are expected to discuss their stance on the Sorsby ruling in the coming days. The NCAA may also intervene to address the concerns surrounding college athletics integrity.
