**Executive Summary**: George Russell secured a controversial pole position for the Austrian GP, escaping investigation for a yellow flag incident on his pole lap.

## Context / Background
The Austrian GP qualifying session saw a dramatic turn of events as Max Verstappen crashed late in the session, prompting a double yellow flag. However, George Russell was not investigated for potentially failing to slow down under the yellow flags, sparking controversy among fans and competitors alike.

> The FIA has explained the delay in double yellow flags, stating that it was due to the location of the incident and the need to ensure driver safety.

## What's Next?
The Austrian GP is set to take place on Sunday, with George Russell starting from pole position. Max Verstappen will start from second place, and the two drivers are expected to battle it out for the top spot on the podium.
