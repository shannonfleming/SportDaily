**England's victory over Mexico in the World Cup has been overshadowed by an unfortunate injury to midfielder Jordan Henderson, who suffered a wrist injury during post-match celebrations.**

## Context / Background
The injury occurred when Henderson fell over advertising hoardings while celebrating England's win. The incident has raised concerns about the safety of players during celebrations.
