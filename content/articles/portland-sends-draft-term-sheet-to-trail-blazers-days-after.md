**Executive Summary**: The city of Portland has sent a draft term sheet to the Trail Blazers, a significant development in the ongoing discussions about the team's arena, the Moda Center. This move comes after the NBA stated that talks between the two parties were 'off track'.

## Context / Background
The relationship between the city of Portland and the Trail Blazers has been under scrutiny, particularly regarding the renovation and funding of the Moda Center. The city's decision to send a draft term sheet indicates a proactive approach to resolving the impasse.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Category | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Draft Term Sheet | Sent by the city of Portland to the Trail Blazers |
| NBA Stance | Talks between the city and the team were previously described as 'off track' |
| Moda Center | The arena in question, requiring renovation and funding discussions |

> The city's move to send a draft term sheet is a crucial step in the negotiations, aiming to find common ground and push the talks forward.

## What's Next?
The Trail Blazers' response to the draft term sheet will be pivotal. The team's stance on the proposed terms will either accelerate the resolution process or lead to further negotiations. The NBA's involvement in ensuring a favorable outcome for both parties is also anticipated.
