**Paul Merson has criticized Liam Rosenior's approach to the game, suggesting that the Chelsea coach needs to stop being so honest with his players when they are struggling.**
## Context / Background
Paul Merson's comments come as Chelsea face a tough battle to qualify for the Champions League next season. With the team currently struggling, Merson believes that Rosenior's approach is not helping the situation.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
Merson specifically mentioned Rosenior's tendency to give players notes and tell them to "respect the ball" even when the team is far behind in a game. For example, when Chelsea was 8-2 down, Merson felt that this approach was not effective.
> I think Liam Rosenior needs to stop saying things like 'respect the ball' and giving players notes when they are 8-2 down. It's just not working.
## What's Next?
Chelsea's upcoming fixtures will be crucial in determining their chances of qualifying for the Champions League. Merson's comments may spark a change in approach from Rosenior, but it remains to be seen how the team will respond.
