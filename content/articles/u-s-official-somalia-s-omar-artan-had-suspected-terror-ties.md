**US Denies Entry to Somali Referee Omar Artan Due to Suspected Terror Ties**

## Context / Background
The US has denied entry to Somali referee Omar Artan, who was set to officiate in the upcoming World Cup, due to suspected terror ties. This decision has sparked controversy and has been met with criticism from various quarters.
