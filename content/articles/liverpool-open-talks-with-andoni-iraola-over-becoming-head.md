**Liverpool has initiated discussions with Andoni Iraola to potentially fill the head coach position.** 
## Context / Background
The search for a new head coach at Liverpool has been ongoing, and the latest development involves talks with Andoni Iraola, a former manager of Bournemouth. This move comes after the departure of the previous head coach and indicates the club's effort to find the right candidate for the role.

## What's Next?
Liverpool is expected to continue discussions with Andoni Iraola, with the aim of finalizing the head coach position soon. The club's decision will be closely watched, as the new head coach will play a significant role in shaping the team's future.
