**England Test captain Ben Stokes is weighing his options regarding his future in the team after a recent nightclub incident.**

## Context / Background
The incident occurred when Stokes and teammate Gus Atkinson breached the England team's new midnight curfew and were involved in an altercation with a Saracens rugby player at a nightclub.

> The England team has a new midnight curfew in place, which Stokes and Atkinson breached, leading to the incident.

## What's Next?
The England team will be monitoring the situation closely and will make a decision on Stokes' future in the coming days. Stokes' consideration of his future could have significant implications for the team's upcoming matches.
