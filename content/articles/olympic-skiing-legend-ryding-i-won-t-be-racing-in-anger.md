**Dave Ryding, a Winter Olympian, has announced his retirement from professional skiing after competing in his fifth and final Games.**

## Context / Background
Dave Ryding reached the end of his skiing career in Bormio on Monday, placing 17th in the men's slalom. This marks the end of an illustrious career for the Olympic skiing legend.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
Ryding's performance in Bormio was a testament to his dedication and perseverance. Although he did not achieve a podium finish, his 17th place ranking is a notable achievement in the competitive world of Olympic skiing.

> 'I won't be racing in anger again' - Dave Ryding's words signify the end of an era in Olympic skiing.

## What's Next?
As Ryding hangs up his skis, the skiing community will undoubtedly miss his presence on the slopes. The upcoming skiing events will be closely watched to see who will fill the void left by this legendary athlete.
