## Context / Background
The recent withdrawal of funding by Saudi Arabia has sent shockwaves through the golf community, with many questioning the future of LIV Golf. This development comes after a series of high-profile defections from the PGA Tour to LIV Golf, which was seen as a major challenge to the traditional golf establishment.
