**England's batting woes continued as they were bowled out for a paltry total, handing New Zealand a convincing 253-run victory in the second Test.**
## Context / Background
The second Test between England and New Zealand was expected to be a closely contested affair, with both teams looking to gain the upper hand in the series. However, it was the Kiwis who dominated the proceedings, with their bowlers wreaking havoc on the English batting lineup.

> The Black Caps' bowlers were relentless in their attack, with Matt Henry being the pick of the bowlers, finishing with impressive figures.

## What's Next?
The two teams will now head to The Oval for the third and final Test of the series, with England looking to salvage some pride and New Zealand aiming to wrap up the series.
