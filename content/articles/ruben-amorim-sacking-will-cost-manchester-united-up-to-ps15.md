## Context / Background
The decision to sack Ruben Amorim comes as a surprise to many, given the Portuguese manager's reputation and the investment Manchester United made in hiring him. The club's hierarchy has deemed it necessary to make a change, however, and are now facing the financial consequences of their decision.
