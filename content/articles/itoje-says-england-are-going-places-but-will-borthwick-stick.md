**England's Six Nations campaign may have been disappointing, but Maro Itoje remains optimistic about the team's future prospects.**

## Context / Background
England recently concluded their worst Six Nations campaign, losing four matches in the tournament. Despite this, key player Maro Itoje believes the team is on the right track and has a positive outlook for their future performances.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The team's performance in the Six Nations has raised questions about the coaching staff, particularly Steve Borthwick's position as head coach. Itoje's comments suggest that the players are committed to improving and moving forward.

## Key Quote
> England are going places, despite the results not going our way in this tournament.

## What's Next?
As England looks to the future, the question on everyone's mind is whether Steve Borthwick will remain in charge. The team's poor performance in the Six Nations has put pressure on the coaching staff, and changes may be on the horizon.
