**Nike has unveiled the 2026 WNBA Rebel Edition uniforms, empowering teams to express their identity.**
## Context / Background
The WNBA has partnered with Nike to launch the Rebel Edition uniforms for the 2026 season. This move allows teams to express their unique identities and showcase their personalities on the court.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The uniforms feature a mix of bold colors and sleek designs, giving each team a distinct look. Some teams, such as the Golden State Valkyries, have already unveiled their black uniforms, while others have showcased their vibrant colors.

## Key Quote
> The Rebel Edition uniforms are a testament to the WNBA's commitment to self-expression and individuality.

## What's Next?
The 2026 WNBA season is set to kick off soon, with all 15 teams donning their new Rebel Edition uniforms. Fans can expect an exciting season with a mix of talented players and stylish uniforms.
