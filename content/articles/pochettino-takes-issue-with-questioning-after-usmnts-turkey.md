**USMNT coach Mauricio Pochettino has defended his team after their loss to Turkey, stating that they had already won their group.**

## Context / Background
The USMNT suffered a late defeat to Turkey in their recent World Cup match. Despite the loss, the team had already secured their spot in the next round by winning their group. 

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The match saw the USMNT create several scoring opportunities, but they were unable to capitalize on them. Turkey, on the other hand, took advantage of a late mistake to score the winning goal.

## Key Quote
> 'Sorry guys, we won (the group)' - Mauricio Pochettino

## What's Next?
The USMNT will look to bounce back from their loss as they move on to the next round of the World Cup. Their next match is scheduled to take place soon, and they will be looking to secure a spot in the quarterfinals.
