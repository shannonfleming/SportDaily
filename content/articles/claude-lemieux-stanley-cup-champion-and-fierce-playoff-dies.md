**Claude Lemieux, a fierce playoff competitor and 4-time Stanley Cup champion, has passed away at the age of 60.**

## Context / Background
Claude Lemieux was a Canadian professional ice hockey player who played in the National Hockey League (NHL) for 21 seasons. He won four Stanley Cup championships with the Montreal Canadiens, New Jersey Devils, and Colorado Avalanche.
