**Jacob Bridgeman has secured his maiden PGA Tour victory at The Genesis Invitational, holding off a late charge from Rory McIlroy to win by one shot.**

## Context / Background
The Genesis Invitational has once again provided an exciting finale to a golf tournament, with Jacob Bridgeman emerging victorious. Bridgeman had a significant lead earlier in the final round but had to fend off a late surge from Rory McIlroy to claim his first PGA Tour win.
