**Chelsea has taken disciplinary action against Enzo Fernández, dropping him for the next two games.** 
## Context / Background
The decision comes after Fernández made comments about living in Madrid, which were perceived as crossing a line by the club. Chelsea's management has decided that the midfielder will miss the upcoming games against Port Vale and Manchester City.
