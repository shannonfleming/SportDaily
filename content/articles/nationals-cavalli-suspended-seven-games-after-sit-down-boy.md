**Cade Cavalli, the Nationals' pitcher, has been suspended for seven games after a brawl sparked by his comment.**
## Context / Background
The incident occurred during a game between the Nationals and the Red Sox, with Cavalli's comment 'Sit down, boy' directed towards Willson Contreras leading to a heated exchange and a benches-clearing brawl.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Player | Team | Suspension Length |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cade Cavalli | Nationals | 7 games |
| Willson Contreras | Red Sox | Suspended |

> 'The comment was unacceptable and Cavalli has apologized, but the suspension is a necessary step to maintain order in the game.'

## What's Next?
The suspension will be served during the upcoming games, and Cavalli will be eligible to return to the lineup after serving his suspension. The incident has sparked a wider conversation about player conduct and the need for respect between teams.
