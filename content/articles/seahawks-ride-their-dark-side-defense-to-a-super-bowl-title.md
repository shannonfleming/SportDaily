## Executive Summary
The Seattle Seahawks have made history by winning Super Bowl LX, overpowering the New England Patriots 29-13. This victory marks a significant milestone for the Seahawks, who rode their formidable 'Dark Side' defense to claim the championship title. The team's defense was instrumental in stifling the Patriots' offense, led by quarterback Drake Maye, and paved the way for their decisive win.

## How the Seahawks' Defense Dismantled the Patriots
The Seahawks' 'Dark Side' defense was in top form, putting immense pressure on the Patriots' offense and limiting their scoring opportunities. The defense was able to contain Drake Maye, who had been a key player for the Patriots throughout the season. The Seahawks' strategy of blitzing and using defensive backs to shut down the Patriots' receivers proved to be highly effective, as they were able to hold the Patriots to just 13 points.
