**The Champions League last-16 draw is set to take place this Friday, and Premier League clubs are eagerly awaiting the announcement of their opponents.**

## Context / Background
The UEFA Champions League is one of the most prestigious club competitions in the world, featuring the top teams from Europe's premier leagues. The last-16 stage is the first knockout round, where teams will face each other in a two-legged tie.

## What's Next?
The last-16 ties are scheduled to take place in February and March, with the first legs being played on February 14-15 and 21-22, and the second legs on March 7-8 and 14-15.
