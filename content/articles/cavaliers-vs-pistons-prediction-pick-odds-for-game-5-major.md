**The Cleveland Cavaliers and Detroit Pistons are set to face off in Game 5 of their playoff series, with the winner taking a crucial step towards advancing to the next round.**

## Context / Background
The Cavaliers and Pistons have been evenly matched throughout the series, with each team trading victories. The Cavaliers will be looking to bounce back from a tough loss in Game 4, while the Pistons will be seeking to build on their momentum.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The key to the game will be the performance of the Cavaliers' star players, including Donovan Mitchell and Evan Mobley. The Pistons, on the other hand, will be relying on the likes of Cade Cunningham and Jalen Duren to lead the charge.

> The Cavaliers have been struggling with injuries, which could be a major factor in the outcome of the game.

## What's Next? (Schedule)
