**Jannik Sinner has advanced to the semi-finals of the Madrid Open after defeating teenager Rafael Jodar.**
## Context / Background
The Madrid Open has seen some thrilling matches this season, with top players competing for the top spot. Jannik Sinner, the current World No 1, has been on a roll, showcasing his exceptional skills on the court.

## Tactical Key Points
Sinner's victory over Jodar was a testament to his skill and experience. The match ended with a score of 6-2 7-6 (7-0), with Sinner dominating the tiebreaker. This win makes him the sixth player to reach the semi-finals of all nine ATP 1000 Masters tournaments.

> Sinner held off 'incredible' teenager Jodar to win the quarter-final match.

## What's Next?
Sinner will now face his next opponent in the semi-finals, looking to continue his winning streak and claim the Madrid Open title.
