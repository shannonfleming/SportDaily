**Ilia Malinin's Olympic dreams were shattered as he fell twice in the men's figure skating final, allowing Mikhail Shaidorov to claim the gold medal.**

## Context / Background
Ilia Malinin, known as the 'Quad God', was a strong contender for the gold medal in the men's figure skating event at the Olympics. However, his performance in the free skate was marred by two falls, which significantly impacted his overall score.
