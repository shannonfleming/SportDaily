**The San Antonio Spurs have pulled even in their series against the Oklahoma City Thunder, thanks in large part to the outstanding performance of Victor Wembanyama.**

## Context / Background
The Spurs were looking to bounce back from a tough loss in Game 3, and they did just that with a dominant performance in Game 4. Victor Wembanyama set the tone early with a half-court heave that sparked the team's offense.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Player | Points | Rebounds | Assists |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Victor Wembanyama | 25 | 10 | 5 |
| De'Aaron Fox | 30 | 5 | 7 |

> The Spurs' defense was the key to their victory, holding the Thunder to just 40% shooting from the field. As Victor Wembanyama said, 'Our defense was the difference maker tonight.'

## What's Next?
The series is now tied at 2-2, with Game 5 set to take place on May 28. The Spurs will look to build on their momentum and take a crucial lead in the series.
