**Zinedine Zidane is set to take over as the new France boss after the World Cup, replacing current manager Didier Deschamps.**

## Context / Background
The French national team has been under the management of Didier Deschamps since 2012. However, with the World Cup approaching, there have been speculations about Deschamps' future with the team. Zidane, a former French player and Real Madrid manager, has been rumored to be the top candidate to replace Deschamps.

## What's Next?
The World Cup is set to take place later this year, and France will be one of the top contenders for the title. With Zidane taking over after the tournament, the team will look to build on their current success and make a strong run in the next European Championship.
