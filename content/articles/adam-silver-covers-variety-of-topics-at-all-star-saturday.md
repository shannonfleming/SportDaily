**Executive Summary**: NBA Commissioner Adam Silver has addressed various topics, including tanking and potential changes to the draft structure, in an effort to improve the league's competitiveness and integrity.

## Context / Background
The NBA has been grappling with the issue of tanking, where teams intentionally lose games to secure better draft picks. Commissioner Adam Silver has been exploring ways to address this problem, including potential changes to the draft structure.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
Some of the key points discussed by Silver include:
* Tanking and its impact on the league's competitiveness
* Potential changes to the draft structure, including taking away picks from tanking teams
* The need to realign incentives to discourage tanking

> The NBA is considering all remedies to address the issue of tanking, including changes to the draft structure.

## What's Next?
The NBA will continue to explore ways to address the issue of tanking and improve the league's competitiveness. Silver's comments suggest that the league is open to making significant changes to the draft structure and other rules to achieve this goal.
