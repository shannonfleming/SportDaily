**Novak Djokovic has been sensationally knocked out of the French Open by Brazilian teenager Joao Fonseca in a thrilling five-set match.**
## Context / Background
The French Open, one of the most prestigious tennis tournaments in the world, has seen its fair share of upsets this year. However, few could have predicted the downfall of Novak Djokovic, a tennis legend, at the hands of a relatively unknown Brazilian player, Joao Fonseca.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Player | Sets Won | Games Won |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Novak Djokovic | 2 | 23 |
| Joao Fonseca | 3 | 26 |
Djokovic, known for his exceptional skill and strategy on the court, seemed to struggle against Fonseca's aggressive playing style. Fonseca's ability to capitalize on Djokovic's mistakes ultimately led to his victory.
> The crowd was on the edge of their seats as Fonseca took the final set, securing his place in the next round of the tournament.
## What's Next?
With Djokovic out of the running, the French Open has become even more unpredictable. Fans will be eagerly watching to see how the remaining players perform, especially Joao Fonseca, who has made a name for himself with this stunning upset.
