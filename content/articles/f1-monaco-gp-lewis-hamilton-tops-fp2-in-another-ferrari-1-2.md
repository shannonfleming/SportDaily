**Lewis Hamilton has taken the top spot in the second free practice session for the Monaco Grand Prix, with Ferrari's Charles Leclerc coming in second.**

## Context / Background
The Monaco Grand Prix is one of the most prestigious events in the Formula 1 calendar, with its narrow streets and tight corners providing a unique challenge for drivers. This year's event has seen a strong performance from Ferrari, with Charles Leclerc dominating the first practice session.

## What's Next?
The third free practice session will take place on Saturday, followed by qualifying. The main event will be held on Sunday, with all eyes on the top drivers as they battle it out for the top spot on the podium.
