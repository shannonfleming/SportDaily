**Executive Summary**: Kansas State has fired college basketball coach Jerome Tang after a disastrous season, with the team suffering an 'embarrassing' blowout. The decision comes amid a struggling season, and the university has named an interim head coach.

## Context / Background
The firing of Jerome Tang is a significant move for Kansas State, as the team has been struggling to find its footing this season.
