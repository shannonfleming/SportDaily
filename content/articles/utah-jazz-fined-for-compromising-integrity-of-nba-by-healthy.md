**The Utah Jazz have been fined by the NBA for compromising the integrity of the league by benching healthy players.**
## Context / Background
The NBA has been dealing with the issue of tanking, where teams intentionally lose games to improve their chances of getting a higher draft pick. The Utah Jazz and several other teams, including the Pacers, have been accused of tanking by benching healthy players.
