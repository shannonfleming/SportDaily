**The San Antonio Spurs have forced a decisive Game 7 in the Western Conference finals after a dominant performance against the Oklahoma City Thunder.**

## Context / Background
The Spurs were facing elimination in Game 6, but they responded with a resounding win to extend the series. The team's performance was led by Victor Wembanyama, who had a standout game after not speaking to the media following Game 5.

## What's Next?
The series will now head to a winner-takes-all Game 7, with the winner advancing to the NBA Finals. The Thunder will look to bounce back from the loss, while the Spurs will aim to ride their momentum to a series win.
