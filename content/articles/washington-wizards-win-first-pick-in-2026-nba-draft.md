## Context / Background
The NBA draft lottery is an annual event where the 14 non-playoff teams from the previous season participate in a weighted lottery to determine the top four picks in the upcoming draft. The Washington Wizards, having finished with one of the worst records in the league, had a high chance of securing a top pick.
