    **The golfing world has been left reeling after Rory McIlroy's public outburst against Bryson DeChambeau over a penalty row at the Open Championship.**
    ## Context / Background
    The incident occurred during the 2026 Open Championship, with McIlroy expressing his frustration at what he perceived as DeChambeau's attempts to 'hold the Open hostage' over a penalty dispute. This is not the first time the two golfers have been at odds, with their on-course rivalry becoming increasingly heated in recent years.
