**Kyle Walker has announced his retirement from international football, bringing an end to a successful career with the England national team.**

## Context / Background
Kyle Walker, the experienced defender, has decided to hang up his boots on the international stage after earning 96 caps for England. This decision comes as a surprise to many, especially with the World Cup just around the corner.
