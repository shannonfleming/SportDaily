**Bruno Guimaraes has informed Newcastle of his desire to join Arsenal, marking a significant development in the summer transfer window.**

## Context / Background
The news comes after Guimaraes' early exit from the World Cup with Brazil, prompting the midfielder to reassess his club future. Newcastle, having invested heavily in their squad, faces the challenge of retaining key players amidst interest from top-tier clubs like Arsenal.

> Although no official statement has been released by the player, sources close to the matter confirm Guimaraes' preference for a move to Arsenal, citing his ambition to play in the Champions League and compete at the highest level.

## What's Next?
The transfer saga is expected to unfold in the coming weeks, with Newcastle likely to negotiate the terms of any potential deal. Arsenal, under pressure to strengthen their midfield, will be keen to secure Guimaraes' signature, but the Magpies are in a strong position to demand a significant fee for their captain.
