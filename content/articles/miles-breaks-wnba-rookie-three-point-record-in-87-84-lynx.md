**Olivia Miles made history by breaking the WNBA rookie three-point record in the Minnesota Lynx's 87-84 victory over Golden State.**

## Context and Background
The WNBA has seen its fair share of talented rookies over the years, but Olivia Miles is certainly making a name for herself. With her recent performance, she has broken the rookie three-point record, a feat that showcases her exceptional shooting skills.
