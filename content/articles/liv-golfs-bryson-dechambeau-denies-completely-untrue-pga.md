**Bryson DeChambeau has denied claims that he has been in talks with the PGA Tour, describing the rumors as 'completely untrue'**. This comes at a time when LIV Golf, the tour DeChambeau is currently a part of, faces significant uncertainty following the withdrawal of Saudi funding.

## Context / Background
LIV Golf has been making headlines recently due to its financial struggles after Saudi Arabia pulled its funding. This move has put the future of the tour and its star players, including Bryson DeChambeau, in jeopardy. The golfing world is watching closely as LIV Golf attempts to secure new funding and stabilize its operations.

## Statement from Bryson DeChambeau
> 'I am committed to LIV Golf and have not been in discussions with the PGA Tour. The rumors are completely untrue, and I look forward to continuing to compete at the highest level with LIV Golf.'

## What's Next?
As LIV Golf navigates its financial challenges, players like Bryson DeChambeau will be under scrutiny. The future of LIV Golf and its ability to attract and retain top talent will depend on its success in securing new funding and establishing a stable financial foundation.
