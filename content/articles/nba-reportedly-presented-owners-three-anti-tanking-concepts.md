**The NBA has taken a significant step towards preventing tanking by presenting owners with three anti-tanking concepts.** 
## Context / Background
The NBA has been struggling with the issue of tanking, where teams intentionally lose games to improve their chances of getting a higher draft pick. This has led to a decrease in the competitiveness of games and a negative impact on the overall fan experience.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The three anti-tanking concepts presented to the owners all involve bringing play-in or playoff teams into the mix. This could potentially lead to more competitive games and a more exciting playoff experience. 
| Concept | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Concept 1 | Play-in tournament featuring teams that just missed the playoffs |
| Concept 2 | Playoff teams earning the right to draft higher based on performance |
| Concept 3 | Combination of play-in tournament and playoff team draft incentives |

> The NBA is committed to finding a solution to the tanking problem, and these concepts are a step in the right direction.

## What's Next?
The NBA will continue to discuss and refine these concepts, with the goal of implementing a new system that promotes competitiveness and fairness. The league will work with owners, players, and other stakeholders to ensure that the new system is effective and beneficial for all parties involved.
