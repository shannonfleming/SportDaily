**Arne Slot has turned down an offer from Fulham for an immediate return to the Premier League, according to reports.**

## Context / Background
The news comes after Slot was recently sacked by Liverpool. Despite being given the opportunity to return to management quickly, Slot has opted not to take the Fulham job.

Fulham will continue their search for a new manager, while Slot will take time to consider his next move. The Premier League is set for an exciting summer of transfers and managerial changes.
