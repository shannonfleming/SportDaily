**Argentina secured a thrilling 3-2 victory over Egypt in the World Cup, but the match was marred by controversy over officiating decisions.**

## Context / Background
The World Cup has been filled with exciting matches, but the Argentina vs. Egypt game has sparked controversy. Egypt's head coach, Hossam Hassan, has expressed dissatisfaction with the refereeing decisions, claiming they were 'unfair' and that the tournament is 'directed towards' reigning champions Argentina.
