**The San Antonio Spurs have taken a 2-1 lead in their NBA playoff series against the Minnesota Timberwolves.**
## Context / Background
The Spurs won Game 3 with a score of 115-108, thanks in large part to a strong performance by Victor Wembanyama. The series is now shifting in favor of the Spurs, who will look to build on their momentum in the next game.

| --- | --- |

The Spurs' victory was fueled by Wembanyama's impressive outing, which included a number of key plays down the stretch. The Timberwolves, on the other hand, struggled to contain Wembanyama and will need to find a way to slow him down if they hope to get back in the series.

> The Spurs' win was a testament to their depth and resilience, as they were able to overcome a strong effort from the Timberwolves.

## What's Next?
The series will continue with Game 4, which is scheduled to take place on a date to be determined. The Spurs will look to build on their lead and take a commanding 3-1 advantage, while the Timberwolves will try to even the series at 2-2.
