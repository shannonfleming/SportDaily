**Tragedy struck the Grand National Meeting as Gold Dancer was put down after winning the Novices' Chase.**

## Context / Background
The Grand National Meeting is one of the most prestigious events in the horse racing calendar, attracting thousands of spectators to Aintree. This year's event has been marred by the tragic incident involving Gold Dancer, who won the Novices' Chase but was put down shortly after due to a severe injury.
