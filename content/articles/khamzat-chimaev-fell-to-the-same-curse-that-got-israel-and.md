**Khamzat Chimaev's loss to Sean Strickland at UFC 328 has sparked a lot of debate in the MMA community.**
## Context / Background
Khamzat Chimaev was on a roll before his fight against Sean Strickland at UFC 328. He was considered one of the top contenders in the middleweight division and was expected to win his fight against Strickland. However, things did not go as planned, and Chimaev lost the fight via decision.

## What's Next?
The loss to Strickland has raised questions about Chimaev's future in the middleweight division. According to Dana White, Chimaev will be changing weight classes after his loss to Strickland.
