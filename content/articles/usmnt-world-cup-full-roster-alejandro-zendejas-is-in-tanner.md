**The United States Men's National Team (USMNT) has announced its full roster for the upcoming World Cup, with Alejandro Zendejas making the cut and Tanner Tessmann missing out.**

## Context / Background
The USMNT, led by coach Mauricio Pochettino, has been preparing for the World Cup with a series of friendlies and training sessions. The final 26-man squad has been highly anticipated, with several players vying for a spot.
