**Radko Gudas, a defenseman for the Anaheim Ducks, has been handed a 5-game suspension by the NHL for kneeing Toronto Maple Leafs star Auston Matthews.**

## Context / Background
The incident occurred during a recent game between the Ducks and the Maple Leafs. Matthews suffered a significant injury from the hit and is now out for the season. The suspension was handed down after a review of the incident by the NHL's Department of Player Safety.

> The NHL's decision to suspend Gudas for 5 games has been met with criticism from some, including Matthews' agent, who has called the suspension 'laughable and preposterous'.

## What's Next?
The Ducks will have to navigate the next 5 games without Gudas, which could impact their chances of making a push in the standings. The Maple Leafs, meanwhile, will have to find a way to replace Matthews' production for the remainder of the season.
