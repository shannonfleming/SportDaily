**The debate surrounding Mohamed Salah's potential move to the MLS has intensified after Don Garber, the MLS commissioner, hit back at an Egyptian executive who warned the player against making the switch.**

## Context / Background
The Egyptian executive had cautioned Salah against joining the MLS, citing concerns about the league's competitiveness and potential impact on the player's career. However, Don Garber has since responded, highlighting the growth and development of the MLS in recent years.
