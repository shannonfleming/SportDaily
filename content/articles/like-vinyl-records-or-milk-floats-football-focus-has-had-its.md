**Football Focus, a long-running BBC program, is set to come to an end after 52 years on the air.**
## Context / Background
The show has been a staple of British football culture, providing news, analysis, and discussion on the sport. However, the BBC has decided to axe the program, citing a need for change and a new approach to covering football.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The decision to end Football Focus has been met with disappointment from fans and former presenters, who have expressed their sadness at the loss of a beloved institution. The show has been on the air since 1974 and has become a part of many fans' weekend routines.

> The axing of Football Focus is a sad day for football fans, and it will be missed by many who have grown up watching the show.

## What's Next?
The BBC has not announced a replacement for Football Focus, but it is expected that a new show will be launched to take its place. The corporation has stated that it is committed to continuing to provide high-quality coverage of football, and that the end of Football Focus is an opportunity to try new things and approach the sport in a fresh way.
