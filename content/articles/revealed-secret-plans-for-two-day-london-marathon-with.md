**The London Marathon is considering a major overhaul by potentially extending the event to two days in 2027, accommodating 100,000 runners.**

## Context / Background
The London Marathon has seen a significant increase in demand, with over 1.1 million runners entering the public ballot for the 2026 race. This surge in interest has prompted organizers to explore new ways to manage the high demand and provide more opportunities for participants.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Year | Number of Runners | Event Duration |
|------|------------------|---------------|
| 2026 | 50,000            | One Day       |
| 2027 | 100,000           | Two Days      |

> The potential two-day event would allow for a significant increase in the number of participants, providing more opportunities for runners to take part in the iconic event.

## What's Next?
The plans for the two-day London Marathon in 2027 are still in the development stage, and organizers will need to work closely with stakeholders, including runners, sponsors, and local authorities, to ensure the success of the new format.
