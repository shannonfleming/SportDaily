**Garry Sobers, a cricket legend and one of the greatest all-rounders in the history of the sport, has passed away at the age of 89.**
## Context / Background
Garry Sobers was a West Indian cricketer who played for the West Indies cricket team from 1954 to 1974. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest cricketers of all time, known for his exceptional skills as a batsman, bowler, and fielder.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
Sobers' impressive career statistics include 8032 runs and 235 wickets in Test cricket. He was also an excellent fielder, taking 109 catches in his Test career.
> Sobers was a true legend of the game, and his passing will be deeply felt by the cricket community.

As the cricket world mourns the loss of Garry Sobers, fans and players alike will be reflecting on his incredible career and the impact he had on the sport.
