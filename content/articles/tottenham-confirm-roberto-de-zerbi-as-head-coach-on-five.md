**Tottenham Hotspur have confirmed the appointment of Roberto De Zerbi as their new head coach on a five-year contract.**
## Context / Background
The Italian manager's appointment comes after a thorough search process by the club, with De Zerbi becoming the third head coach of the season for Spurs. His experience and tactical acumen are expected to bring stability and success to the team.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
De Zerbi's managerial career has been marked by his ability to implement a possession-based style of play, which has yielded positive results in his previous roles. His stats and achievements will be closely watched as he begins his tenure at Tottenham.
> The club's decision to appoint De Zerbi has been met with a mixed reaction from fans and pundits alike, with some questioning the choice and others expressing optimism about the future.
## What's Next?
De Zerbi will be expected to hit the ground running, with a busy schedule of Premier League and cup fixtures on the horizon. His first match in charge will be a crucial test of his tactics and ability to motivate the squad.
