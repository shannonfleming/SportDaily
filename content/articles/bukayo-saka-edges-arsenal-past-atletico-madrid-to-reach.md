The Champions League semi-final second leg between Arsenal and Atlético Madrid was highly anticipated, with Arsenal looking to capitalize on their away goal advantage from the first leg. The Gunners' home stadium, the Emirates, was electric as the team took to the pitch to face their Spanish opponents.
