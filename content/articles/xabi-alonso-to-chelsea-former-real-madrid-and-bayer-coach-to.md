**Xabi Alonso is set to become the new head coach of Chelsea, with the club announcing that he has agreed a four-year contract.**
## Context / Background
The former Real Madrid and Bayer Leverkusen coach has been in talks with Chelsea for several weeks, and it appears that a deal has been reached. Alonso, who played for Liverpool and Real Madrid during his playing career, has been coaching since 2018 and has gained a reputation as a talented young coach.
