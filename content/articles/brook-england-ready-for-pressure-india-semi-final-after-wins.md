**England is gearing up to face India in the T20 World Cup semi-final, with captain Harry Brook expressing confidence in his team's ability to handle pressure.**

## Context / Background
The upcoming semi-final match between England and India is highly anticipated, with both teams having shown impressive performances in the tournament so far. England's captain, Harry Brook, believes that his team's experience in navigating 'niggly' games earlier in the tournament will be beneficial in their high-stakes match against India.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
England's past performances have demonstrated their ability to remain calm under pressure, which will be crucial in their match against the host nation. The team's strategy and key players will be essential in determining the outcome of the semi-final.

## Key Quote
> Captain Harry Brook feels England coming through 'niggly' games earlier in the T20 World Cup will stand them in good stead for their blockbuster semi-final against host nation India.

## What's Next?
The semi-final match between England and India is scheduled to take place on Thursday in Mumbai. The outcome of this match will determine which team advances to the final.
