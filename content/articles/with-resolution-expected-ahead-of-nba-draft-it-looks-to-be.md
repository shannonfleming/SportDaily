**Executive Summary**: The highly anticipated Giannis Antetokounmpo trade has reportedly narrowed down to two teams: the Boston Celtics and the Miami Heat. With the NBA Draft looming, a resolution is expected soon.

The Milwaukee Bucks' superstar has been at the center of speculation, with several teams vying for his services. However, it appears that the Celtics and Heat have emerged as the frontrunners in the pursuit of the two-time NBA MVP.
