**LeBron James has decided to leave the Los Angeles Lakers in pursuit of 'complete happiness', according to his agent Rich Paul.**

## Context / Background
LeBron James has been with the Lakers since 2018 and has led the team to multiple championships. However, it seems that he is now looking for a new challenge and a chance to find 'complete happiness' in his career.
