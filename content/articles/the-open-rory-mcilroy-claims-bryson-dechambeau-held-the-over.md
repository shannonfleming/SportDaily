**Rory McIlroy has strongly criticized Bryson DeChambeau for his role in a rules drama delay at The Open 2026, stating that DeChambeau 'held the tournament hostage' with his actions.**

## Context / Background
The incident occurred during the third round of The Open, where DeChambeau was involved in a rules controversy that led to a significant delay in the tournament. McIlroy, who is also competing in the event, expressed his frustration with the situation, labeling DeChambeau's behavior as 'performative'.
