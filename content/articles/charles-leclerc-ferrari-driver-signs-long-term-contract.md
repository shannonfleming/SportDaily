**Charles Leclerc has committed his future to Ferrari by signing a long-term contract extension with the team.**

## Context / Background
Charles Leclerc, the Ferrari driver, has been with the team since 2019 and has shown immense promise, becoming a key figure in the team's push for the championship. The extension of his contract is a significant move for Ferrari, as it secures the services of one of the most talented drivers in the sport.
