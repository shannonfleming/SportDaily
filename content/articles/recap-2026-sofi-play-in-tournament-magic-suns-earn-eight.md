**The 2026 SoFi Play-In Tournament has concluded, with the Orlando Magic and Phoenix Suns earning the eighth seeds in their respective conferences.**

## Context / Background
The NBA play-in tournament is a relatively new format that allows teams to compete for the final playoff spots in each conference. This year's tournament featured several exciting matchups, with the Magic and Suns ultimately emerging victorious.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Team | Opponent | Score |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Orlando Magic | Charlotte Hornets | 115-108 |
| Phoenix Suns | Golden State Warriors | 125-110 |

> The Suns' victory over the Warriors marked the end of Golden State's season, while the Magic's win over the Hornets secured their spot in the playoffs.

## What's Next?
The Magic and Suns will now move on to the first round of the playoffs, where they will face tougher competition. The Suns are set to play the Oklahoma City Thunder, while the Magic's opponent has not been determined.
