**Tottenham Hotspur's Champions League journey has come to an end, but the team can hold their heads high after a strong second leg against Atletico Madrid.**

## Context / Background
Tottenham Hotspur faced Atletico Madrid in the Champions League, with the Spanish side having the upper hand after the first leg. Despite this, Spurs showed resilience and determination in the second leg, pushing their opponents to the limit.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The match saw both teams create numerous scoring opportunities, with Spurs eventually winning 3-2 on the night. However, it was not enough to overturn the deficit from the first leg, and Atletico Madrid progressed to the next round with a 5-7 aggregate score.

## Key Quote
> 'We can be proud of our performance, but we are disappointed to be out of the competition.' - Igor Tudor

## What's Next?
Tottenham Hotspur will now focus on their domestic campaign, aiming to finish the season on a high note. Meanwhile, Atletico Madrid will prepare for their next Champions League match, looking to continue their European journey.
