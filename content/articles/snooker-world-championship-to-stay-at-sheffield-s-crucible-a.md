**The Snooker World Championship has secured its home at Sheffield's Crucible Theatre until 2045, with the venue set to undergo a major refurbishment.**

## Context / Background
The Crucible Theatre has been the home of the Snooker World Championship since 1977, hosting the tournament for over four decades. The new deal ensures that the championship will remain at the iconic venue for at least another 20 years.
