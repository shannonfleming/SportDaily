**The NCAA has announced a major expansion of the men's and women's basketball tournaments, increasing the number of teams from 68 to 76 starting next season.**

## Context / Background
The decision to expand the tournament field is aimed at providing more opportunities for teams to participate in the postseason. This change is expected to have a significant impact on the college basketball landscape.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Category | Current | New |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Number of Teams | 68 | 76 |
| Tournament Format | Single-elimination | Single-elimination |

> The expansion of the tournament field is a positive development for college basketball, as it will allow more teams to compete and provide additional opportunities for student-athletes.

## What's Next?
The new 76-team format is set to debut next season, with the exact schedule and bracket to be announced at a later date. Fans can expect a more exciting and unpredictable tournament experience with the added teams.
