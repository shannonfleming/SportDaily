**Chicago Bears legend Steve 'Mongo' McMichael has been diagnosed posthumously with CTE after dying of complications from ALS.**

## Context / Background
Steve McMichael, a legendary figure in the NFL, played for the Chicago Bears and was a key member of their Super Bowl XX championship team. His diagnosis with CTE, a brain disease linked to repeated head trauma, has sparked concern and discussion about player safety in the NFL.

The diagnosis of CTE in Steve McMichael is a reminder of the importance of monitoring and addressing the health and well-being of athletes, particularly those in high-impact sports like football.
