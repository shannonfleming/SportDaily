**Georges St-Pierre has praised Sean Strickland for his upset victory over Khamzat Chimaev, calling Strickland the 'better fighter' in the matchup.**

The UFC middleweight division has seen a significant shift in power dynamics with Sean Strickland's surprise win over Khamzat Chimaev. This victory marks a turning point in Strickland's career and has garnered attention from veteran fighters like Georges St-Pierre.
