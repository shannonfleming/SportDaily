**Pep Guardiola has called for respect and understanding after a Ramadan break was booed during Manchester City's win at Leeds.**
## Context / Background
The incident occurred during Manchester City's recent win at Leeds, where a pause in the game to allow Muslim players to break their Ramadan fast was met with booing from some sections of the crowd. This reaction has been widely condemned by the football community, with many calling for greater respect and understanding.
