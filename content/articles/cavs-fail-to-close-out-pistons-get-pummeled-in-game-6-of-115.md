**The Cleveland Cavaliers' hopes of advancing to the next round of the NBA playoffs were put on hold as they suffered a devastating loss to the Detroit Pistons in Game 6 of the Eastern Conference semifinals.**

## Context / Background
The Cavaliers were looking to close out the series with a win in Game 6, but the Pistons had other plans. With their backs against the wall, the Pistons came out strong, determined to force a Game 7.
