**Sabastian Sawe has made history by becoming the first man to run a marathon in under two hours, achieving a world record time of 1:59:30 at the London Marathon.**

## Context / Background
The London Marathon is one of the most prestigious marathons in the world, attracting top athletes from around the globe. This year's event saw Sabastian Sawe make history by breaking the two-hour barrier, a feat that many thought was impossible.
