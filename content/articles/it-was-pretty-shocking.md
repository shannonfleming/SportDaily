**Lando Norris has expressed his disappointment with McLaren's performance in the British Grand Prix, describing it as 'pretty shocking' despite finishing fourth.**

## Context / Background
The British Grand Prix saw Lando Norris finish in a solid fourth position, but the McLaren driver was not satisfied with the team's overall pace. Norris believes that McLaren have 'a hell of a lot to improve' if they want to compete with the top teams like Mercedes and Ferrari.
