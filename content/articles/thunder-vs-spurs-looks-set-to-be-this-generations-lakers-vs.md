**The NBA playoffs have witnessed a resurgence in the rivalry between the Oklahoma City Thunder and the San Antonio Spurs, drawing comparisons to the iconic Lakers vs. Celtics matchups of the past.**

## Context / Background
The Thunder and Spurs have been engaging in a tightly contested series, with the Thunder managing to even the series in Game 2, thanks in part to the outstanding performance of Shai Gilgeous-Alexander, who scored 30 points. This series is being closely watched by NBA fans, given the historical significance of the rivalry between these two teams.
