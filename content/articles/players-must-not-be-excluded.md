**The England and Wales Cricket Board and all eight Hundred franchises have reaffirmed their commitment to an inclusive competition, stating that no player should be excluded based on their nationality.**
## Context / Background
The statement comes as a reassurance to the cricket community, emphasizing the importance of diversity and inclusivity in the sport.
## Tactical Key Points
The ECB and franchises are united in their stance, ensuring that players from all nations are welcome to participate in The Hundred.
> The Hundred is a unique competition that brings together talented players from around the world, and we are committed to maintaining an inclusive environment.
## What's Next?
The upcoming season of The Hundred is expected to feature a diverse range of players, with the ECB and franchises working together to promote a culture of inclusivity.
