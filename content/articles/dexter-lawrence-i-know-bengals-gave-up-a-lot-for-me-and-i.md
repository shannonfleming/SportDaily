**Dexter Lawrence has spoken out about his recent trade to the Cincinnati Bengals, expressing his gratitude towards the team for giving up a significant draft pick to acquire him.**
## Context and Background
The Bengals traded their number 10 overall draft pick to the New York Giants in exchange for Dexter Lawrence, a move that has sent shockwaves through the NFL community. This trade has significant implications for both teams, with the Bengals bolstering their defensive line and the Giants gaining a valuable draft pick.
## Tactical Key Points and Stats
The Bengals' decision to trade for Lawrence is likely a strategic move to strengthen their defense, which has been a weakness in recent seasons. With Lawrence on board, the Bengals will be looking to make a deep playoff run in the upcoming season.
> "I know the Bengals gave up a lot for me, and I don’t take that for granted," Lawrence said in a recent interview.
## What's Next?
The NFL draft is just around the corner, and the Giants will be looking to use their newly acquired draft pick to select a top talent. The Bengals, on the other hand, will be focused on integrating Lawrence into their team and preparing for the upcoming season.
