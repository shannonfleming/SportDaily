**Carlos Alcaraz's title defence at the French Open has come to an end before it even began, as the Spaniard has withdrawn from the tournament due to a wrist injury.**

## Context / Background
The French Open, one of the most prestigious tennis tournaments in the world, is set to kick off soon. Carlos Alcaraz, the current title holder, was expected to be one of the top contenders for the championship. However, a wrist injury has forced him to pull out of the tournament, leaving a significant gap in the competition.
