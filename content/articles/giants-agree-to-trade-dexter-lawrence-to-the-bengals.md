**The New York Giants have made a significant move by trading defensive tackle Dexter Lawrence to the Cincinnati Bengals in exchange for the No. 10 overall pick in the 2026 NFL Draft.**

## Context and Background
The trade comes as a surprise to many, given Lawrence's importance to the Giants' defense. However, the team's decision to trade him may be a strategic move to strengthen their position in the upcoming draft.

## Tactical Key Points
The Bengals, on the other hand, are looking to bolster their defensive line with the addition of Lawrence. The 25-year-old defensive tackle has been a key player for the Giants since being drafted in 2019.

> The Giants' decision to trade Lawrence is likely a move to acquire more draft picks and build for the future.

## What's Next?
The 2026 NFL Draft is scheduled to take place from April 23-25. The Giants will look to use their newly acquired pick to select a talented player to fill the void left by Lawrence.
