**The NBA is set to introduce new anti-tanking rules, as announced by Commissioner Adam Silver, in an effort to maintain competitive balance across the league.**
## Context / Background
The NBA has been considering anti-tanking measures for some time, aiming to prevent teams from deliberately losing games to secure better draft picks. This move is expected to have a significant impact on the league's competitive dynamics.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The new rules are intended to discourage teams from tanking by introducing penalties for teams that finish with the worst records. While the specifics of the rules have not been fully disclosed, it is clear that the NBA is committed to ensuring a level playing field for all teams.
> The introduction of anti-tanking rules is a positive step for the NBA, as it will help maintain the integrity of the game and promote competitive balance.
## What's Next?
The new anti-tanking rules are expected to come into effect next season, with the NBA working to finalize the details in the coming months. Fans can expect a more competitive league, with teams incentivized to perform at their best throughout the season.
