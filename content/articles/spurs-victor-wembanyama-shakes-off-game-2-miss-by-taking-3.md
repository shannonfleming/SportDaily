**The San Antonio Spurs have bounced back in the NBA Finals, thanks to a dominant performance by Victor Wembanyama in Game 3.**
## Context / Background
The Spurs were facing a must-win situation after dropping the first two games of the series. However, Wembanyama's impressive display has given them new life in the championship series.
