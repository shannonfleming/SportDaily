**Alpine has successfully cleared the first hurdle in their appeal against the penalties given to Pierre Gasly during the Monaco Grand Prix.**
## Context / Background
The Monaco Grand Prix was marred by controversy after several drivers, including Gasly, were given penalties for pit-lane speeding. Alpine has since appealed the decision, citing an error on the part of the FIA.
