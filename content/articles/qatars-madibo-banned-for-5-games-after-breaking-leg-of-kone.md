    **Qatar's Assim Madibo has been handed a 5-game ban by FIFA following a severe foul on Canada's Ismael Kone during their World Cup match on June 18, 2026.**
    ## Context / Background
    The incident occurred during a World Cup match between Qatar and Canada, which Canada won 6-0. The foul was severe enough to break Kone's leg, sparking widespread outrage and calls for Madibo to face disciplinary action.

    > The ban is a clear message that such behavior will not be tolerated in the sport.
    ## What's Next?
    Qatar will have to navigate their upcoming matches without Madibo, who will be serving his 5-game suspension. Canada, on the other hand, will be looking to build on their impressive 6-0 win.
