**LIV Golf is facing a major crisis as tensions escalate following the withdrawal of Saudi funding.** 
## Context / Background
The golf world has been embroiled in controversy since the emergence of LIV Golf, a rival tour backed by Saudi Arabia's Public Investment Fund. The new tour has attracted several high-profile players, but its impact on the traditional golf tours has been a subject of debate.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Tournament | LIV Golf | Traditional Tours |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Funding | Withdrawn | Stable |
| Player Participation | Decreasing | Increasing |

> The withdrawal of Saudi funding has left LIV Golf stars facing a painful road back to traditional tours, with some being branded 'selfish' for their decision to join the rival tour.

## What's Next?
As the golf world struggles to come to terms with the implications of LIV Golf's crisis, fans and players alike are left wondering what the future holds for the sport. With the traditional tours seemingly unaffected by the emergence of LIV Golf, it remains to be seen how the rival tour will recover from this significant setback.
