**The Philadelphia Flyers have made a significant move in the NHL by tendering an offer sheet to Ducks star Leo Carlsson, which would make him the league's highest-paid player.**

## Context / Background
The move comes as a surprise to many, given the current state of the NHL and the salary cap. However, the Flyers are clearly looking to make a statement and bring in a top talent to their roster. Carlsson, who has been a key player for the Ducks, would undoubtedly be a significant addition to the Flyers' lineup.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The offer sheet is reportedly a 5-year, $90 million deal, which would be the largest contract in NHL history. This move could have significant implications for the league, as it could set a new benchmark for player contracts.

> The Flyers' offer sheet is a bold move, and it will be interesting to see how the Ducks respond. 

## What's Next?
The Ducks now have the opportunity to match the offer sheet and keep Carlsson on their roster. If they choose not to match, the Flyers will acquire Carlsson's services and become a more formidable team in the NHL.
