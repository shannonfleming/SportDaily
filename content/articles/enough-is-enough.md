**Ferrari team boss Frederic Vasseur has expressed his frustration with the constant changes to Formula 1's race-start procedure, stating that "enough is enough".**

## Context / Background
The Ferrari team has been vocal about their dissatisfaction with the current state of Formula 1's race-start procedure. With numerous changes made in recent times, the team believes it's time for stability and consistency.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The constant changes have led to confusion among teams and drivers, affecting their performance on the track. A stable race-start procedure would allow teams to focus on their strategy and execution.

> Frederic Vasseur's statement highlights the growing concern among F1 teams regarding the frequent changes to the race-start procedure.

## What's Next?
The F1 community awaits the response from the governing body regarding the concerns raised by Ferrari. A meeting is expected to take place soon to discuss the issue and potential solutions.
