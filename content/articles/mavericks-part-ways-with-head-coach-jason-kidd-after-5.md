**The Dallas Mavericks have announced that they are parting ways with head coach Jason Kidd after five seasons.** 
## Context / Background
The decision comes as a surprise, given that Kidd had recently signed a lucrative deal to continue as the team's head coach. However, it appears that the team's management has decided to go in a different direction.

## What's Next?
The Mavericks will now begin their search for a new head coach. The team will be looking for someone who can lead them to consistent success and potentially a championship.
