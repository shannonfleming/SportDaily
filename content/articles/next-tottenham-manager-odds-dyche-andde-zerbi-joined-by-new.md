**Tottenham Hotspur is on the hunt for a new manager after parting ways with Igor Tudor**, who left the club after just seven games in charge. The search for his replacement has been ongoing, with several names being considered for the position.

## Context and Background
Tottenham's decision to let go of Tudor was made in an effort to save the team from relegation. The club has been struggling in the Premier League, and a change in management was deemed necessary to turn things around. With several experienced managers being considered, the club is looking for someone who can lead them to safety and potentially challenge for a spot in the top half of the table.

## Tactical Key Points and Stats
The two top contenders for the job are Sean Dyche and Roberto De Zerbi. Both managers have a proven track record of success in the Premier League, with Dyche having managed Burnley and De Zerbi currently managing Brighton. The decision will ultimately come down to who the club believes can best lead them to success.

> The club is looking for a manager who can bring stability and success to the team. With several experienced managers being considered, the decision will not be an easy one.

## What's Next?
The club is expected to make a decision on the new manager in the coming days. Until then, the team will continue to prepare for their upcoming matches under the guidance of an interim manager. Fans will be eagerly awaiting the announcement, hoping that the new manager will be able to turn the team's fortunes around.
