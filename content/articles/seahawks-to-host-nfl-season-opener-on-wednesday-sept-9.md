**The Seattle Seahawks are set to host the NFL season opener on Wednesday, Sept. 9, marking the second time the league will open on a Wednesday.**

## Context / Background
The NFL has announced that the 2026 season will kick off on a Wednesday for the second time in its history, with the champion Seahawks hosting the opening game. This move is seen as an effort to create a unique and exciting start to the season.
