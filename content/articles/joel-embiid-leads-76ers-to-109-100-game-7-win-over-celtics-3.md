**The Philadelphia 76ers have made history by coming back from a 3-1 deficit to win their series against the Boston Celtics, with Joel Embiid leading the charge in Game 7.**
## Context / Background
The 76ers were on the brink of elimination but managed to turn their season around, thanks in large part to the dominant play of Joel Embiid. The Celtics, who were without Jayson Tatum due to a calf injury, were unable to close out the series.

> The 76ers' victory marks the first time they have come back from a 3-1 deficit since 1982.
## What's Next?
With the series win, the 76ers will move on to face the New York Knicks in the next round of the playoffs. The Celtics, on the other hand, will head into the offseason, looking to regroup and come back stronger next year.
