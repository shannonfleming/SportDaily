**England has announced its squad for the upcoming friendlies against Uruguay and Japan, with several notable inclusions and omissions.**

## Context / Background
The England national team is gearing up for two high-profile friendlies against Uruguay and Japan. These matches will serve as crucial preparation for the team ahead of major tournaments. Coach Thomas Tuchel has named a 35-man squad, which includes uncapped players James Garner and Jason Steele.

> The omission of Trent Alexander-Arnold from the squad has been described as a 'tough decision' by Coach Tuchel, indicating the competitiveness within the team.

## What's Next?
The friendlies against Uruguay and Japan will be played in the coming weeks. These matches will provide an opportunity for the players to impress Coach Tuchel and stake their claim for a spot in the team for future tournaments.
