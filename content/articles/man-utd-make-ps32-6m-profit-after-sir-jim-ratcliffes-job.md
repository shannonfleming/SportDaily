**Manchester United has announced a profit of £32.6m for the latest financial period, benefiting from the cost-cutting measures implemented by Sir Jim Ratcliffe.**

## Context / Background
The financial results come after a period of restructuring under the new ownership. Manchester United's latest financial reports indicate a significant profit despite a fall in revenues. This development suggests that the club's efforts to streamline its operations and reduce costs are yielding positive results.
