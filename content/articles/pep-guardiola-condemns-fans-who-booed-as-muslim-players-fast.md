**Pep Guardiola has strongly condemned the actions of fans who booed as Muslim players broke their Ramadan fast during a match against Leeds.** 
## Context / Background
The incident occurred during a recent Premier League match between Leeds and Manchester City. The game was paused to allow Muslim players to break their Ramadan fast, but some fans in attendance booed the stoppage.
