    **Vincent Kompany has rejected multiple offers to work as a pundit during the upcoming World Cup, a move that will undoubtedly disappoint broadcasters and fans alike who were eager to gain insight into the mind of one of football's most respected figures.**

## Context / Background
Vincent Kompany, the former Manchester City captain, has been in high demand since his retirement from professional football. His experience and knowledge of the game make him an ideal candidate for punditry work, especially on the biggest stage in international football - the World Cup.
