**Craig Morton, a legendary NFL quarterback, has passed away at the age of 83. He was the first quarterback to start a Super Bowl for two different franchises, the Dallas Cowboys and the Denver Broncos.**

## Context / Background
Craig Morton had a remarkable career in the NFL, playing for the Dallas Cowboys, New York Giants, and Denver Broncos. He was known for his exceptional leadership skills and his ability to perform under pressure.
