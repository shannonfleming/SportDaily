**Mexico has made history by ending their 40-year knockout drought in the World Cup, defeating Ecuador to advance to the next stage.**
## Context / Background
Mexico's victory over Ecuador marks a significant milestone for the team, as they had not won a knockout stage match in the World Cup since 1986. This win is a testament to the team's hard work and determination.
