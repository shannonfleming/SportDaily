**Jake Paul is looking to make a statement in his next boxing match by targeting a bout with former UFC heavyweight champion Francis Ngannou.**

## Context / Background
Jake Paul has been making waves in the boxing world with his unorthodox style and willingness to take on high-profile opponents. His latest target is Francis Ngannou, the former UFC heavyweight champion who recently made his professional boxing debut.

## Tactical Key Points
Paul believes he has the skills to take down Ngannou, citing his own boxing experience and Ngannou's limited time in the ring. Paul also referenced Anthony Joshua's knockout victory over Ngannou, saying he can replicate that success.

> I think he is a terrible boxer, I'll knock him out like Anthony Joshua did.

## What's Next?
The potential bout between Paul and Ngannou is still in the works, but Paul is confident that he can secure the win. With his sights set on Ngannou, Paul is looking to prove himself as a serious contender in the boxing world.
