**Arsenal is looking to bounce back from recent cup losses, with Mikel Arteta and David Raya urging the team to use these setbacks as motivation.** 
## Context / Background
Arsenal has faced significant challenges in recent cup matches, with the team seeking to regain momentum. Mikel Arteta and David Raya are emphasizing the importance of perspective and using losses as fuel for upcoming games.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The team's performance will be crucial in the upcoming Champions League tie against Sporting. Arteta has called for his players to maintain focus and draw lessons from their recent losses.
> 'We need to use these experiences to grow and improve,' said a source close to the team, reflecting the sentiment of Arteta and Raya.
## What's Next?
Arsenal is set to face Sporting in the Champions League, followed by a match against Bournemouth. The team will need to navigate these challenges while dealing with an injury crisis.
