**Trinidad Chambliss has been granted another year of college eligibility by a state judge, allowing him to continue playing for Ole Miss.**
## Context / Background
The news comes after the NCAA initially denied Ole Miss' request for a waiver reconsideration for Trinidad Chambliss. The quarterback had been seeking an additional year of eligibility due to circumstances that affected his ability to play in previous seasons.
