**Tiger Woods has decided not to captain the US Ryder Cup team in 2027, according to the PGA of America.**

## Context / Background
The Ryder Cup is a prestigious international golf tournament played between the United States and Europe. Tiger Woods, one of the most successful golfers of all time, was considered a strong candidate to captain the US team in 2027.
