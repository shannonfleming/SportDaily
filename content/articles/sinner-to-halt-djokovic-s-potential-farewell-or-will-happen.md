## Context / Background
Novak Djokovic, a 23-time Grand Slam champion, is still going strong at 39 years old. However, his career is nearing its end, and this tournament could be one of his last.

> The tennis world is watching to see if Djokovic can continue his winning streak or if Sinner will cause an upset.
## What's Next?
The semi-final match between Djokovic and Sinner is highly anticipated, and fans are eager to see the outcome. The winner will move on to the final, where they will face another tough opponent.
