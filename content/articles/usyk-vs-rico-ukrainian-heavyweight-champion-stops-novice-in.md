**Usyk Defends His Title in a Grueling Match**
## Context / Background
Oleksandr Usyk, the Ukrainian heavyweight champion, faced off against Rico Verhoeven in a highly anticipated match. The event, which took place at the Pyramids of Giza, saw Usyk defend his WBC heavyweight title against the novice Verhoeven.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The match was marked by a series of intense exchanges, with both fighters giving it their all. While Verhoeven put up a good fight, Usyk's experience and skill ultimately paid off, leading to a controversial 11th-round stoppage win.
