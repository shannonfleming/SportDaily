**Trinidad Chambliss has been granted a preliminary injunction by a judge, making him eligible to play in the 2026 season.**
## Context and Background
The NCAA had previously ruled Chambliss ineligible, but the judge's decision overturns this ruling, allowing him to participate in the upcoming season.
## Tactical Key Points
The decision is seen as a significant win for Chambliss and Ole Miss, and could have implications for the NCAA's eligibility rules.
> The judge's ruling is just the tip of the iceberg in the NCAA's eligibility crisis.
## What's Next?
Chambliss is now eligible to play in the 2026 season, and Ole Miss will likely be strengthened by his presence on the field. The NCAA may appeal the decision, but for now, Chambliss is free to compete.
