**Somali referee Omar Artan has been barred from entering the US and will miss the World Cup 2026 tournament.**

## Context / Background
The World Cup 2026 is scheduled to take place in the United States, and Omar Artan, a top African referee, was set to participate in the tournament. However, due to vetting concerns, Artan has been denied entry into the US.
