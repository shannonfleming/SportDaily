**Italy has announced that Aaron Nola will be the starting pitcher for their World Baseball Classic semifinal match against Venezuela, opting for him over Michael Lorenzen.**

## Context / Background
The World Baseball Classic has reached its semifinal stage, with Italy set to face off against Venezuela. This matchup promises to be intense, with both teams vying for a spot in the championship game against the United States.

Given Venezuela's undefeated record in the tournament so far, Italy's choice of starting pitcher is crucial. Aaron Nola, with his experience and skill, is expected to give Italy a competitive edge.

> The decision to start Aaron Nola reflects Italy's strategy to use their best assets to overcome the challenging Venezuelan team.

## What's Next?
The winner of the Italy vs. Venezuela semifinal will proceed to the championship game, where they will face the United States. The stakes are high, and both teams are preparing to give it their all.
