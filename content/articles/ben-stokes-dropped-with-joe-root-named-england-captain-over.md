**Ben Stokes has been dropped from the England squad for the second Test against New Zealand, with Joe Root taking over as captain.**

The decision comes after Stokes was involved in a nightclub incident, leading to his omission from the team. Harry Brook was also considered for the captaincy but ultimately, Joe Root was chosen to lead the team.
