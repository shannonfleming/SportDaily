**The Anaheim Ducks have expressed their disagreement with the icing wave-off decision in their Game 1 loss to the Vegas Golden Knights.**

## Context / Background
The Ducks lost the first game of the series 3-1, with the Golden Knights taking the lead in the second round of the 2026 Stanley Cup Playoffs. The disputed icing wave-off call has sparked controversy, with the Ducks arguing that it affected the outcome of the game.
