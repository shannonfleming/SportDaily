**The Los Angeles Lakers are on the brink of elimination after falling to the Oklahoma City Thunder in Game 3 of their playoff series.**
## Context / Background
The Lakers, led by LeBron James, were expected to be a strong contender in the NBA playoffs. However, they have struggled against the Thunder, who have taken a 3-0 lead in the series.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The Thunder's defense has been a key factor in their success, holding the Lakers to under 100 points in each of the first three games. The Lakers, on the other hand, have struggled to find their rhythm on offense, with James being the only consistent scorer.
> The Lakers are playing as well as they can — and it doesn't matter against the Thunder.
## What's Next?
The Lakers will look to avoid elimination in Game 4, which is scheduled to take place on May 12. They will need to find a way to overcome the Thunder's strong defense and get their offense clicking if they hope to stay alive in the series.
