**Thierry Henry has come to the defense of his former club Arsenal and their manager Mikel Arteta, amidst a backdrop of neutrals opposing the team's potential title win.**
## Context / Background
The Premier League is nearing its climax, with Arsenal being one of the top contenders for the title. However, Thierry Henry believes that many neutrals are not in favor of Arsenal winning the title, instead wanting 'anyone but Arsenal' to take the trophy.

## Tactical Key Points
Henry's comments come as a response to criticism of Arsenal's style of play under Mikel Arteta. He praised Arteta's ability to find a winning formula, which could potentially end Arsenal's 22-year wait for a title.

> Neutrals don't want Arsenal to win, they want anyone but Arsenal to win.

## What's Next?
As the season unfolds, Arsenal will face intense competition from other top teams. Their performance in the remaining matches will be crucial in determining their chances of winning the title.
