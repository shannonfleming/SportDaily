**PSG has been slammed for requesting a match postponement due to their upcoming Champions League tie against Liverpool.**

## Context / Background
PSG has requested that their Ligue 1 match against Lens be postponed due to their Champions League quarter-final tie against Liverpool. The French club has cited the short turnaround time between the two matches as the reason for their request.

> PSG's request has been met with criticism from various quarters, with some labeling it as a 'troubling' attempt to gain an unfair advantage.

## What's Next?
The decision on whether to grant PSG's request is yet to be made. If the request is denied, PSG will have to play their Ligue 1 match against Lens as scheduled, before taking on Liverpool in the Champions League.
