**The Dallas Mavericks have announced the departure of head coach Jason Kidd after five seasons with the team.**
## Background
The decision to part ways with Kidd comes as a surprise, given his tenure with the Mavericks. Kidd had been at the helm since 2021 and led the team to the playoffs in his first season.

| 2025-2026 | 42 | 40 | .512 |
> The Mavericks' front office has decided to go in a new direction, and we respect that decision. - Jason Kidd
## What's Next?
The Mavericks will now begin their search for a new head coach. The team is expected to consider several candidates, including experienced coaches and up-and-coming assistants.
