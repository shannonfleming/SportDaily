**The English Football League (EFL) has released a statement regarding the Sky Bet Championship Play-Off Final, which may be delayed due to the ongoing Spygate hearing.**
## Context / Background
The Sky Bet Championship Play-Off Final is scheduled to take place, but the EFL has announced that it may be delayed due to the Spygate hearing, which involves allegations of spying against Southampton. The hearing may affect the participation of Southampton in the play-off final.
