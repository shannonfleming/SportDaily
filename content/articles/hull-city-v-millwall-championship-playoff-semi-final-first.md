**The stage is set for an intense matchup as Hull City takes on Millwall in the first leg of the Championship playoff semi-final.**

## Context / Background
Hull City and Millwall are set to clash in the first leg of the Championship playoff semi-final. The winner of this two-legged tie will move one step closer to securing a spot in the Premier League.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
Both teams have had impressive runs in the Championship, with Hull City finishing 4th and Millwall finishing 5th. The head-to-head record between the two teams is evenly matched, with both teams winning one game each in their last two encounters.

## Key Quote
Millwall boss Alex Neil has called on his team to produce a 'moment of magic' to earn a first-leg advantage.

> 'We need to be at our best and produce a moment of magic to win the game.'

## What's Next?
The second leg of the semi-final will take place on May 12th, with the winner advancing to the Championship playoff final.
