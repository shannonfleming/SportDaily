**The College Football Playoff (CFP) is facing criticism as it rushes towards expansion to 24 teams.** 
## Context / Background
The proposal to expand the CFP to 24 teams has been gaining momentum, with many coaches and experts backing the move. However, some critics argue that the expansion is being rushed and may lead to unforeseen consequences.
