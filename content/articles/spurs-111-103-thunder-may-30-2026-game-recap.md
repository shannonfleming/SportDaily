**The San Antonio Spurs have secured their spot in the NBA Finals with a 111-103 victory over the Oklahoma City Thunder.**
## Context / Background
The Spurs' win marks a significant milestone in their season, as they prepare to face off against the Knicks in the NBA Finals. This rematch of the 1999 NBA Finals is highly anticipated, with the Spurs looking to bring home the championship title.
