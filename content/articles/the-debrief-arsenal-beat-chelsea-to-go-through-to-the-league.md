## Arsenal's Road to Glory: Beating Chelsea to Reach the League Cup Final
Executive Summary:
In a thrilling encounter, Arsenal managed to overcome Chelsea, securing their spot in the League Cup final. This victory marks a significant milestone for the Gunners, who have been working tirelessly to shed their "nearly men" tag. With this win, Arsenal has broken a six-year hoodoo, reaching their first final in six years. The team's determination and perseverance have paid off, and they are now one step closer to claiming the coveted League Cup title.

## How Arteta's Tactics Outsmarted Potter's Chelsea
Arsenal's success can be attributed to the clever tactics employed by manager Mikel Arteta. The Spanish tactician's decision to start with a strong lineup, including the likes of Bukayo Saka and Gabriel Martinelli, paid dividends as the duo caused chaos for the Chelsea defense. Meanwhile, Chelsea's Graham Potter struggled to find the right balance, and his team's lack of cohesion ultimately led to their downfall.
