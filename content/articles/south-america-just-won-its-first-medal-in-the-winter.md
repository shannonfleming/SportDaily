**South America has made history by winning its first-ever Winter Olympics medal, courtesy of Brazil's Lucas Pinheiro Braathen.**

## Context / Background
The Winter Olympics have long been dominated by nations from the Northern Hemisphere, with South American countries struggling to make an impact. However, Brazil's Lucas Pinheiro Braathen has changed the narrative by winning the gold medal in the giant slalom event.
