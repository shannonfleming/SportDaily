**The Travelers Championship has been delayed due to lightning, resulting in a Monday morning playoff between Scottie Scheffler and Viktor Hovland.**

## Context / Background
The Travelers Championship, one of the most prestigious golf tournaments, has reached its climax with Scottie Scheffler and Viktor Hovland emerging as the top contenders. The tournament, known for its challenging course and unpredictable weather, has once again lived up to its reputation with a lightning delay forcing the playoff into Monday morning.
