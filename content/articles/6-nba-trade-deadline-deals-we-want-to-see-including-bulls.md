## What the Thunder, Blazers, and Raptors Need to Do to Make a Playoff Push
The Oklahoma City Thunder, Portland Trail Blazers, and Toronto Raptors are all teams that are on the cusp of making a playoff push. However, each team has its own unique needs and weaknesses that need to be addressed. The Thunder need a consistent scorer to pair with Shai Gilgeous-Alexander, while the Blazers require a defensive stopper to complement Damian Lillard. The Raptors, on the other hand, need a reliable rebounder to support Pascal Siakam.
