**Arsenal have progressed to the semifinals of the Women's Champions League despite losing 1-0 to Chelsea in the second leg of their quarterfinal tie.**

## Context / Background
The Gunners had won the first leg 2-1 at home, and their aggregate score of 3-2 was enough to see them through to the next round. Chelsea, the holders of the competition, put up a good fight but ultimately fell short.

## What's Next?
Arsenal will now face the winners of the other quarterfinal ties in the semifinals. The draw for the next round is set to take place soon, and the Gunners will be eager to find out who their opponents will be.
