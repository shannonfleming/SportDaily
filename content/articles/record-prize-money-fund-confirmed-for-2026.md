**Wimbledon has announced a record increase in prize money for the 2026 tournament, with a 20% rise from the previous year.**

## Context / Background
Wimbledon, one of the most prestigious tennis tournaments in the world, has confirmed a record prize money fund for the 2026 tournament. This increase is a significant move to address player discontent and rising costs.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The 20% increase in prize money is a substantial boost to the tournament's overall purse. While the exact figures have not been disclosed, it is expected to be one of the highest in tennis history.

> The increase in prize money is a welcome move for players, who have been calling for better compensation for their participation in the tournament.

## What's Next?
The 2026 Wimbledon tournament is set to take place in the summer, with the exact dates to be confirmed. The record prize money fund is expected to attract top players from around the world, making for a highly competitive and exciting tournament.
