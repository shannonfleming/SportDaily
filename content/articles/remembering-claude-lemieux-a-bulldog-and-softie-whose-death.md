**Claude Lemieux, a four-time Stanley Cup champion, has passed away at the age of 60, leaving the NHL community in shock and mourning.**

## Context / Background
Lemieux was a renowned hockey player known for his tenacious and physical playing style, earning him the nickname 'bulldog' among fans and peers. Throughout his career, he played for several teams, including the Montreal Canadiens, New Jersey Devils, and Colorado Avalanche, winning four Stanley Cup championships.
