**LIV Golf is facing an uncertain future after Saudi Arabia's Public Investment Fund (PIF) announced it will end funding of the rebel golf league at the end of the season.**
## Context and Background
The decision comes as a significant blow to LIV Golf, which has been trying to establish itself as a major player in the world of golf. The league has been backed by Saudi Arabia's PIF since its inception and has attracted several high-profile golfers, including Jon Rahm and Bryson DeChambeau.
## Tactical Key Points and Stats
* LIV Golf has announced the appointment of a new chairman as part of its efforts to restructure and secure new funding.
* The league has also announced strategic board appointments and an expanded strategy, but the loss of Saudi Arabia's funding is a significant setback.
* Several LIV Golf members have reportedly reached out to the PGA Tour about a potential return, but the terms and pathways for such a move are expected to be more restrictive.
> The future of LIV Golf is uncertain, and it remains to be seen how the league will navigate this challenging period.
## What's Next?
LIV Golf will need to secure new funding and find a way to attract and retain top golfers if it is to survive. The league's new chairman and board appointments will play a crucial role in shaping its future strategy.
