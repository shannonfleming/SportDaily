**Southampton manager Tonda Eckert has been accused of authorising a spying scandal, which could lead to disciplinary action.**

## Context / Background
The scandal involves Southampton pressuring staff to spy on opponents, which has been deemed 'deplorable' by the EFL. The League Arbitration Panel has accused the club of a 'determined plan from the top down' to engage in spying activities.
