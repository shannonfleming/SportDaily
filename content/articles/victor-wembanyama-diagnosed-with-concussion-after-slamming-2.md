    **Victor Wembanyama, the Spurs' young star, has been diagnosed with a concussion after a face-slaming incident in Game 2.**
    ## Context / Background
    The incident occurred during Game 2 of the NBA play-offs, where Wembanyama fell and slammed his face on the floor. The young player was immediately taken out of the game and put into concussion protocol.
    ## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
    | Team | Points |
    | --- | --- |
    | Spurs | 90 |
    | Trail Blazers | 95 |
    The Spurs were leading the game before Wembanyama's exit, but the Trail Blazers managed to level the series after his departure.
    > The Spurs' chances of winning the series have been affected by Wembanyama's injury, as he is a key player for the team.
    ## What's Next?
    The Spurs will have to play without Wembanyama in the next game, as he is ruled out due to concussion protocol. The team will have to adjust their strategy to compensate for his absence.
