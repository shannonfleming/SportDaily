**Serena Williams has withdrawn from her upcoming doubles match at Wimbledon, citing an injury. This decision comes as a disappointment to fans who were looking forward to seeing her team up with her sister Venus.**
## Context / Background
The Williams sisters were set to compete in the doubles tournament at Wimbledon, one of the most prestigious tennis events in the world. However, due to Serena's injury, they will no longer be able to participate.
