**Fabio Wardley has expressed his disappointment over the potential fight between Oleksandr Usyk and Rico Verhoeven, stating that it's not a genuine challenge for the boxing champion.**

## Context / Background
The boxing world has been abuzz with the possibility of Oleksandr Usyk facing off against elite kickboxer Rico Verhoeven. However, not everyone is excited about this potential matchup. Fabio Wardley, a prominent figure in the boxing scene, has come out to criticize the idea, stating that Verhoeven would not pose a genuine challenge to Usyk.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
While Verhoeven has an impressive record in kickboxing, his experience in traditional boxing is limited. This lack of experience could be a significant factor in a potential fight against Usyk, who is a highly skilled and experienced boxer.
| Fighter | Boxing Experience | Kickboxing Experience |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Oleksandr Usyk | Extensive | None |
| Rico Verhoeven | Limited | Extensive |

> I'm disappointed that Usyk would even consider fighting someone like Verhoeven. It's not a genuine challenge, and it doesn't do anything to advance Usyk's career or legacy.
