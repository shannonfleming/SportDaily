**Daniel Jones has officially entered free agency, but the Colts have placed a transition tag on the quarterback.** 
## Context / Background
The NFL free agency period has begun, and one of the most notable players available is quarterback Daniel Jones. Despite the Colts' efforts to sign him to a long-term deal, the two sides were unable to reach an agreement. As a result, the Colts have placed a transition tag on Jones, which allows them to match any offer he receives from another team.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Team | Player | Tag |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Colts | Daniel Jones | Transition Tag |

> The transition tag gives the Colts the right to match any offer Jones receives from another team, but it also allows Jones to negotiate with other teams.

## What's Next? (Schedule)
Jones will now be able to negotiate with other teams, but the Colts will have the opportunity to match any offer he receives. The free agency period is expected to be highly competitive, with several teams looking to sign a new quarterback.
