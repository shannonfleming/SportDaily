**Charles Leclerc has topped the final day of the Bahrain pre-season test, setting the fastest time ahead of Lando Norris and Max Verstappen.**

## Context / Background
The Bahrain pre-season test is a crucial part of the Formula 1 calendar, allowing teams to fine-tune their cars and prepare for the upcoming season. This year's test has seen several teams show promising signs, with Ferrari, McLaren, and Red Bull all looking strong.
