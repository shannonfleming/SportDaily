**Constitution Hill, the 2023 Champion Hurdle winner, has been ruled out of the upcoming Champion Hurdle and will instead pursue a career in Flat racing.**

## Context and Background
Constitution Hill has had a remarkable hurdling career, culminating in his Champion Hurdle win in 2023. However, his connections have decided that it is in the best interest of the horse to transition to Flat racing.

## Tactical Key Points
| Category | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Current Status | Ruled out of Champion Hurdle |
| Future Plans | Pursue career in Flat racing |
| Trainer | Nicky Henderson |

> 'It isn't fair to ask him to jump again,' said Nicky Henderson, highlighting the decision to end Constitution Hill's hurdling career.

## What's Next?
Constitution Hill will now focus on preparing for his Flat racing debut. The exact schedule for his first Flat start has not been announced, but it is expected to be later this year.
