**The New England Patriots have made a significant move by releasing wide receiver Stefon Diggs, a decision aimed at saving costs.**

## Context / Background
The Patriots' decision to release Stefon Diggs comes after a single season with the team. Despite having a productive 1,000-yard season and contributing to the team's Super Bowl run, the financial implications of keeping him on the roster were deemed too high.

## What's Next?
With Stefon Diggs now a free agent, several teams, including the Ravens and Steelers, are rumored to be interested in signing him. This move could significantly impact the Patriots' lineup and their chances in the upcoming season.
