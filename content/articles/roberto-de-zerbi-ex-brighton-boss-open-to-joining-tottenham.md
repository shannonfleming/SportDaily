**Roberto De Zerbi is open to joining Tottenham Hotspur as the new head coach immediately, following the sacking of Igor Tudor.** 
## Context / Background
Tottenham Hotspur is in search of a new head coach after parting ways with Igor Tudor. The club has been in talks with Roberto De Zerbi, the former Brighton boss, about taking over the position. De Zerbi has expressed his interest in the job and is considering the opportunity to join the Premier League club.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
De Zerbi's experience in the Premier League with Brighton could be a significant factor in his potential appointment. During his time at Brighton, De Zerbi implemented a possession-based style of play, which led to the team's improvement in the league. 

## What's Next?
Tottenham Hotspur is expected to make a decision on the new head coach soon, with De Zerbi being the heavy favorite for the job. The club is looking to avoid relegation and is hoping that the new coach can turn their season around.

> No official statement has been made by Tottenham Hotspur or Roberto De Zerbi regarding the position.
