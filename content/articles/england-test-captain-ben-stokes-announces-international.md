**England Test captain Ben Stokes has announced his retirement from international cricket, citing it as the best decision for him.**

## Context / Background
Ben Stokes, the renowned English cricketer and current Test captain, has made the surprise announcement to retire from international cricket. This decision comes during the ongoing Test series against New Zealand. Stokes has been a pivotal figure in English cricket, known for his exceptional all-round skills.

> 'Retiring from international cricket is the best thing for me,' said Stokes, emphasizing the need to prioritize his well-being and the demands of the sport.

## What's Next?
With Stokes' retirement, England will need to find a new Test captain. The immediate focus, however, remains on the ongoing series against New Zealand. The decider at Trent Bridge will be crucial for both teams.
