**Switzerland has secured a spot in the World Cup quarterfinals after defeating Colombia in a penalty shootout.**
## Context / Background
The match between Switzerland and Colombia ended in a 0-0 draw after extra time, leading to a penalty shootout. Switzerland's Ruben Vargas scored the decisive penalty, sending his team to the quarterfinals.

## What's Next?
Switzerland will face Argentina in the quarterfinals, a highly anticipated matchup. The schedule for the quarterfinals will be announced shortly.
