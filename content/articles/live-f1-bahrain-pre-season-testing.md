**The highly anticipated F1 pre-season testing is currently underway in Bahrain, with top drivers like Lando Norris and Max Verstappen taking to the track.**
## Context / Background
The F1 pre-season testing in Bahrain marks the beginning of the 2026 Formula 1 season. This testing period is crucial for teams to fine-tune their cars and for drivers to get accustomed to the new vehicles. With several changes in the driver lineup and team personnel, this season is expected to be highly competitive.
