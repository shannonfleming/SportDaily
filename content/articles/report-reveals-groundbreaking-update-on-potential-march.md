**The NCAA is considering a significant expansion of its March Madness basketball tournament, with reports suggesting that the competition could grow to 76 teams.**

## Context / Background
The NCAA has been discussing potential changes to its tournament format for some time, with the aim of increasing participation and revenue. The proposed expansion would see the number of teams competing in the tournament increase from 68 to 76.

| --- | --- |

> The expansion of the NCAA tournament is expected to have a significant impact on college basketball, with more teams and players participating in the competition.

## What's Next?
The NCAA is expected to make a decision on the proposed expansion in the coming months, with the changes potentially coming into effect for the next season.
