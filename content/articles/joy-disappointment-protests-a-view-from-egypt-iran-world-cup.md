**Egypt secured a crucial win in their World Cup tie against Iran, but the match was marred by controversy surrounding a late VAR call.**

## Context / Background
The Egypt-Iran World Cup tie was highly anticipated, with both teams looking to secure a win to boost their chances of advancing in the tournament. The match was also significant due to its coincidence with Seattle's Pride celebrations, which added an extra layer of complexity to the event.
