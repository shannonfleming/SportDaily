## Context / Background
Garret Anderson was a renowned American professional baseball outfielder who played his entire 17-year career in Major League Baseball (MLB) with the California Angels, Anaheim Angels, and Los Angeles Angels of Anaheim. He holds the record for most career hits with the Angels.
