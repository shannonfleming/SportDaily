**The Seattle Seahawks have been sold to a group led by Vinod Khosla, a current NFL owner, for a record price of $9.6 billion.**

## Context / Background
The sale of the Seahawks is a significant development in the NFL, with the team being one of the most valuable franchises in the league. The previous owner, Paul Allen's estate, had been looking to sell the team after his passing.
