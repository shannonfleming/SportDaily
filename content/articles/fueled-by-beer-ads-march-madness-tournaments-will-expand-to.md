**The NCAA has announced a significant expansion of its men's and women's basketball tournaments, increasing the number of participating teams from 68 to 76.**

## Context / Background
The decision to expand the tournament field is reportedly fueled by increased revenue from beer advertisements. This move is expected to have a significant impact on the collegiate basketball landscape, with more teams competing in the postseason.
