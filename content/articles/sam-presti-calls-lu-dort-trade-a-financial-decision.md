**The Oklahoma City Thunder have made a significant move in the NBA, trading Lu Dort to the Atlanta Hawks in a three-team deal that also involves the Dallas Mavericks.**
## Context / Background
The trade has been described by Thunder General Manager Sam Presti as 'a financial decision', highlighting the complex nature of roster management in the NBA. Lu Dort, known for his defensive prowess, will now join the Hawks, while the Mavericks have acquired Zaccharie Risacher.

## What's Next?
The trade will have implications for the upcoming season, with the Hawks looking to integrate Lu Dort into their lineup and the Mavericks hoping to get the best out of Zaccharie Risacher. The Thunder, meanwhile, will focus on rebuilding their roster.
