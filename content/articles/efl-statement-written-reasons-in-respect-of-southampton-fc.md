**The English Football League has released a statement regarding the spying allegations against Southampton FC.**
## Context / Background
The EFL has been investigating the 'Spygate' scandal, in which Southampton head coach Tonda Eckert was accused of authorizing the spying of opponents. The investigation has found that there was a 'determined plan from the top down' to gather information on opposing teams.

Southampton FC has released a statement regarding the League Arbitration Panel appeal update. The club is expected to face disciplinary action, which may include fines or points deductions.
