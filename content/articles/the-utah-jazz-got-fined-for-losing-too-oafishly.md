**The National Basketball Association (NBA) has fined the Utah Jazz and Indiana Pacers for compromising the integrity of the game by tanking.**

## Context / Background
The NBA has been facing issues with tanking, where teams intentionally lose games to secure better draft picks. This practice has been criticized for compromising the integrity of the game and affecting the fan experience.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Team | Fine Amount |
| --- | --- |
| Utah Jazz | Not disclosed |
| Indiana Pacers | Not disclosed |

The NBA has not disclosed the amount of the fines, but it has made it clear that it will not tolerate any behavior that compromises the integrity of the game.

> The NBA is committed to ensuring that all games are competitive and that the integrity of the game is maintained. - Adam Silver, NBA Commissioner

## What's Next?
The NBA will continue to monitor the situation and take action against any team that is found to be tanking. The league is also exploring ways to address the issue of tanking, including changes to the draft system and incentives for teams to compete.
