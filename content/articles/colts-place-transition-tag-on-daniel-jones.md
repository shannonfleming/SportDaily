**The Indianapolis Colts have placed a transition tag on quarterback Daniel Jones, according to recent reports. This move allows the Colts to retain the rights to Jones while also giving them the opportunity to match any offer sheet he may receive from another team.**

## Context / Background
The transition tag is a rarely used designation in the NFL, but it provides teams with a way to maintain control over their players while also allowing them to test the market. In the case of Daniel Jones, the Colts are likely using the transition tag as a way to gauge his value and determine whether he is worth keeping on their roster.

## What's Next?
The Colts will now have to wait and see if any other teams are interested in signing Daniel Jones to an offer sheet. If another team does make an offer, the Colts will have the opportunity to match it and retain Jones' services. If they decline to match, they will receive compensation in the form of draft picks.
