**Marion Bartoli has advised Emma Raducanu to adopt a consistent coaching approach, similar to Aryna Sabalenka's, in order to achieve long-term success in tennis.**

## Context / Background
Marion Bartoli, a former Wimbledon champion and current Sky Sports analyst, has shared her insights on Emma Raducanu's coaching situation. Raducanu has had multiple coaches throughout her career, which has raised concerns about her ability to establish a consistent playing style.

## Tactical Key Points
Bartoli's suggestion is centered around the idea that stability in coaching can lead to improved performance on the court. Aryna Sabalenka's coaching setup has been cited as an example, with Sabalenka having worked with her current coach for an extended period.

## Key Quote
> Former Wimbledon champion and Sky Sports analyst Marion Bartoli has urged Emma Raducanu to take a leaf out of Aryna Sabalenka's coaching manual by sticking with one coach for a long period of time.

## What's Next?
Raducanu's upcoming tournaments will be closely watched, as fans and analysts await her decision on her coaching staff. A consistent coaching approach could potentially lead to improved results for the British star.
