**Brad Stevens has addressed the situation surrounding Jaylen Brown after the team's failed bid to sign Giannis Antetokounmpo.**
## Context and Background
The Boston Celtics recently attempted to sign Giannis Antetokounmpo, which has led to speculation about Jaylen Brown's future with the team. Brown has been a key player for the Celtics, but the team's pursuit of Antetokounmpo has raised questions about their plans for Brown.
## Tactical Key Points
The Celtics' decision to pursue Antetokounmpo has been seen as a sign that the team is looking to make significant changes to their roster. However, Brad Stevens has stated that Jaylen Brown is still a 'big part' of the team's plans.
> 'Jaylen Brown is a big part of our team, and we value him highly. However, we can't predict the future, and we have to make decisions that are best for the team.'
## What's Next?
The Celtics will need to make a decision about Jaylen Brown's future with the team. If they decide to trade him, there are several teams that could be interested in acquiring him.
