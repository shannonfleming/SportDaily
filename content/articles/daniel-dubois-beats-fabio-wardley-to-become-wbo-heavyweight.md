**Daniel Dubois has made history by becoming the new WBO heavyweight champion after defeating Fabio Wardley in an epic 11-round fight.**
## Context / Background
The highly anticipated match between Daniel Dubois and Fabio Wardley took place recently, with both fighters determined to claim the WBO heavyweight title. The bout was an intense and closely contested one, with both fighters giving it their all.

The fight was a bloody and grueling one, with both fighters suffering injuries. However, it was Dubois who emerged victorious, stopping Wardley in the 11th round.
> 'It was a tough fight, but I knew I had the skills and the determination to win,' said Dubois in a post-fight interview.
## What's Next?
With his victory, Dubois has cemented his place as one of the top heavyweight boxers in the world. He will now look to defend his title against other top contenders in the division.
