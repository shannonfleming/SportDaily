**The recent World Cup match has sparked concern among fans and organizers due to the large number of empty seats, with many attributing this to the high ticket prices.**

## Context / Background
The World Cup is one of the most widely viewed and attended sporting events globally. However, the current tournament has seen a significant number of empty seats during matches, which has raised concerns over the pricing strategy for tickets.
