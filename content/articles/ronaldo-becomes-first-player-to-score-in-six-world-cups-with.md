**Cristiano Ronaldo has etched his name in the history books by becoming the first player to score in six World Cups.** 
## Context / Background
The Portuguese forward achieved this feat by scoring against Uzbekistan in the 2026 World Cup. This milestone is a testament to Ronaldo's enduring talent and dedication to the sport. 
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Player | Goals Scored | World Cups Played |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cristiano Ronaldo | 1 | 6 |
Ronaldo's goal-scoring ability has been a key factor in Portugal's success in the tournament. 
> 'This is an incredible achievement, and I'm honored to be the first player to score in six World Cups,' Ronaldo said in a post-match interview.
## What's Next?
Portugal will look to build on this momentum as they face their next opponents in the World Cup. With Ronaldo leading the charge, they will be a force to be reckoned with.
