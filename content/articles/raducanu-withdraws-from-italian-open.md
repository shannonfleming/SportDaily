**Emma Raducanu has withdrawn from the Italian Open due to a post-viral illness, dealing a blow to her clay-court campaign.**
## Context / Background
The Italian Open, a key tournament in the lead-up to the French Open, will now be without one of its top competitors. Raducanu's withdrawal is a significant setback for the British No. 1, who was looking to build momentum on the clay courts.
## Tactical Key Points 
Raducanu's absence from the Italian Open will be felt, given her rising star status in the tennis world. The tournament, which attracts many of the world's top players, will now see other competitors vie for the top spot.
> No quotes are available from Raducanu at this time, but her team confirmed the withdrawal due to illness.
## What's Next?
The Italian Open will continue without Raducanu, with other top players competing for the title. Fans will be watching to see how the tournament unfolds and which players will rise to the top.
