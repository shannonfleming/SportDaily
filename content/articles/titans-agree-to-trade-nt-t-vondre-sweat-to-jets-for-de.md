**The Tennessee Titans and the New York Jets have agreed to a trade that sends NT T'Vondre Sweat to the Jets in exchange for DE Jermaine Johnson.**
## Context / Background
The NFL has seen several significant trades in recent weeks, and this deal between the Titans and the Jets is the latest move that could impact the balance of power in the league. T'Vondre Sweat, a talented nose tackle, will be joining the Jets, while Jermaine Johnson, a promising defensive end, will be heading to the Titans.

> The trade is seen as a strategic move by both teams to strengthen their defensive lines. Sweat's ability to clog up the middle and Johnson's pass-rushing skills will be valuable assets to their new teams.

As the NFL season progresses, the impact of this trade will become clearer. Both teams will be looking to integrate their new players into their systems and make a push for the playoffs.
