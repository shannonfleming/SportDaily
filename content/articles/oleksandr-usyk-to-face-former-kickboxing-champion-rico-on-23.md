**Oleksandr Usyk is set to defend his WBC title against former kickboxing champion Rico Verhoeven on May 23.**
## Context / Background
Oleksandr Usyk, the current WBC heavyweight champion, has confirmed his upcoming fight against Rico Verhoeven, a renowned kickboxer. The match is scheduled to take place in Egypt, with Usyk aiming to defend his title.

## Tactical Key Points
The fight between Usyk and Verhoeven promises to be an exciting encounter, given their different backgrounds in boxing and kickboxing. While Usyk has the experience of being a professional boxer, Verhoeven's kickboxing skills may pose a unique challenge.
