**Formula 1 is facing a critical situation as the ongoing war in the Middle East threatens to cancel its Bahrain and Saudi Arabian Grand Prix races.**

## Context / Background
The current conflict in the Middle East has raised significant concerns about the safety and feasibility of hosting F1 races in Bahrain and Saudi Arabia. With the 2026 calendar already under scrutiny, the potential cancellation of these races could have far-reaching implications for the sport.
