**McLaren is set to investigate the cause of their power unit-related problems that led to Oscar Piastri and Lando Norris being unable to start the Chinese Grand Prix.**
## Context / Background
The Chinese Grand Prix saw McLaren face significant setbacks as both their drivers, Oscar Piastri and Lando Norris, were unable to start the race due to power unit issues. The team is currently unsure about the cause of these problems and has announced plans to investigate the matter further.

## What's Next?
McLaren will be looking to resolve these issues ahead of their next race, ensuring that their drivers can compete without the risk of power unit failures. The investigation into the cause of these problems is a priority for the team.
