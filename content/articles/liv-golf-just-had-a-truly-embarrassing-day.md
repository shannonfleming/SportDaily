**LIV Golf has suffered a major setback with the Asian Tour joining forces with the European-PGA Tour alliance, marking a significant challenge for the organization.**
## Context / Background
The golf world has been witnessing a significant shift in recent times, with various tours and alliances being formed. The latest development sees the Asian Tour joining hands with the European-PGA Tour alliance, a move that is expected to have far-reaching implications for the sport.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Tour | Alliance |
| --- | --- |
| Asian Tour | European-PGA Tour |
| LIV Golf | Independent |
The formation of this alliance is likely to increase competition and create new opportunities for golfers worldwide.
> The Asian Tour's decision to join the European-PGA Tour alliance is a significant blow to LIV Golf, which has been trying to establish itself as a major player in the golf world.
## What's Next?
The future of LIV Golf looks uncertain, with several major golf acquisitions giving us a glimpse into the future of the sport. The PGA Tour, DP World Tour, and Asian Tour alliance is expected to put pressure on LIV Golf, making it challenging for the organization to attract top talent.
