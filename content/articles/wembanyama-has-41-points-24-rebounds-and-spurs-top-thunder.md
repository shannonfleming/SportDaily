**The San Antonio Spurs have taken a crucial 1-0 lead in the Western Conference finals, thanks to a historic performance from Victor Wembanyama.**

## Context / Background
The highly anticipated series between the Spurs and the Oklahoma City Thunder got off to a thrilling start, with the Spurs emerging victorious in a double-overtime thriller. The game saw Victor Wembanyama put on a show, with 41 points and 24 rebounds, leading his team to a 122-115 win.
