## Context / Background
The boxing world has been abuzz with the news of a potential rematch between two of the sport's biggest names, Floyd Mayweather Jr and Manny Pacquiao. The first fight between the two took place in 2015, with Mayweather Jr emerging victorious. Since then, both fighters have continued to compete at the highest level, with Pacquiao recently expressing his desire for a rematch.
