**Jeremiyah Love has made a significant impression at the NFL Combine by posting the second-fastest 40-yard dash time among the fastest RB group in Combine history.**

## Context / Background
The NFL Combine is an annual event where college football players showcase their skills and athletic abilities to NFL coaches, general managers, and scouts. The 40-yard dash is one of the most highly anticipated events, as it measures a player's speed and acceleration.
