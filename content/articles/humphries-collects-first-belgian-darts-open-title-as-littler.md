**Luke Humphries has secured his first Belgian Darts Open title, defeating Jonny Clayton 8-6 in the final.**
## Context / Background
The Belgian Darts Open has been a stage for emerging talent and seasoned players alike, with Luke Humphries and Luke Littler being two of the notable competitors in the recent tournament. Humphries, looking to silence critics, aimed to claim his first stage title since September.

Humphries' victory marks a significant turn in his performance, showcasing his skill and determination. On the other hand, Luke Littler's 12-match winning streak came to an abrupt end with a shock defeat to Niels Zonneveld.
