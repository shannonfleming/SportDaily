**The Kansas City Chiefs have made a significant move in the NFL draft by trading up to select LSU CB Mansoor Delane with the No. 6 pick.**
## Context / Background
The Chiefs traded their No. 9 pick to the Browns in exchange for the No. 6 pick, allowing them to secure the top cornerback prospect in the draft. Mansoor Delane, a highly touted cornerback from LSU, is expected to bring a significant boost to the Chiefs' secondary.
