**The FIA has announced plans to review Formula 1's regulations following a significant incident involving Oliver Bearman at the Japanese Grand Prix.**
## Context / Background
The incident occurred during the Japanese Grand Prix, where Bearman was involved in a crash, highlighting concerns over closing speeds. This has prompted the FIA to take a closer look at the current regulations to ensure driver safety.

## What's Next?
The FIA meetings in April will be crucial in determining the future of F1's regulations. The outcome of these discussions could have a significant impact on the sport, with potential changes aimed at improving driver safety.
