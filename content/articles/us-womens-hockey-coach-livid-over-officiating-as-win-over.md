    **The US women's hockey team has advanced to the semifinals of the Winter Olympics, but their win over Italy was marred by controversy over officiating.**
    ## Context / Background
    The US women's hockey team has been dominating the competition in the Winter Olympics, with their latest win being a 6-0 victory over Italy in the quarterfinals. However, the match was not without controversy, as the US coach was livid over the officiating, claiming that the referees 'blatantly break the rules.'
