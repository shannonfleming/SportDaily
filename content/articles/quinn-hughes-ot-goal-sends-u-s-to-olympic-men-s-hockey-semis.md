**The US men's hockey team has advanced to the Olympic semifinals thanks to an overtime goal from Quinn Hughes.**

## Context / Background
The US team faced off against Sweden in the quarterfinals, with the game going into overtime. The US had been nervous in OT, but Quinn Hughes stepped up to score the winning goal, securing their spot in the semifinals.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The US will now face Slovakia in the semifinals, after beating Sweden 3-2 in OT. The team's win has set up an exciting matchup, with the US looking to continue their success in the tournament.

> Quinn Hughes' overtime goal was the difference-maker in the game, as the US team breathed a sigh of relief after a tense matchup against Sweden.

## What's Next?
The US team will face Slovakia in the semifinals, with the winner advancing to the gold medal game. The team is looking to continue their momentum and bring home a medal.
