**North Carolina has parted ways with men's basketball coach Hubert Davis after five seasons.**
## Context / Background
The decision comes after a historic NCAA Tournament first-round collapse against VCU. Davis had been the head coach of the Tar Heels since 2021.

| 2022-2023 | 20-13 |
| 2023-2024 | 24-10 |
| 2024-2025 | 22-11 |
| 2025-2026 | 18-14 |
> The search for a new coach is expected to begin immediately.
## What's Next? (Schedule)
The Tar Heels will look to find a new coach to lead the team into the next season. Potential candidates to replace Davis have already started to emerge.
