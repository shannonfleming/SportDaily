**Wessel Nijman has made history by winning the Elten Safety Shoes European Darts Trophy with a stunning 8-3 victory over Gerwyn Price in Germany.**
## Context / Background
The European Darts Trophy is a prestigious event that attracts top darts players from around the world. This year's edition saw Wessel Nijman face off against Gerwyn Price in the final, with Nijman emerging victorious.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Player | Sets Won | Legs Won |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Wessel Nijman | 8 | 26 |
| Gerwyn Price | 3 | 17 |
Nijman's impressive performance saw him take control of the match from the outset, ultimately leading to his 8-3 win.
