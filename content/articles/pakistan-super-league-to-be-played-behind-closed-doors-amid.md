**The Pakistan Super League, the country's premier domestic T20 competition, will be played behind closed doors due to the ongoing fuel crisis.**

## Context / Background
The Pakistan Cricket Board announced on Sunday that the league will take place in empty stadiums, citing the recent spike in oil prices as the reason for this decision. This move is expected to have a significant impact on the atmosphere and overall experience of the tournament.

The opening ceremony of the tournament has also been scrapped due to the current situation.

> No official statement has been made regarding the potential return of spectators to the stadiums, but the PCB will continue to monitor the situation.

## What's Next?
The Pakistan Super League is scheduled to continue as planned, albeit behind closed doors. The PCB has assured that all necessary measures are being taken to ensure the smooth operation of the tournament.
