**The Boston Red Sox have made a significant move by firing manager Alex Cora amid a huge coaching shake-up.**

## Context / Background
The Red Sox have had a disappointing start to the season, with a record of 10-17. This move comes as a surprise to many, but the team's performance has been under scrutiny. Cora has been the manager of the Red Sox since 2018 and has led the team to a World Series championship in 2018.

The Red Sox will begin their search for a new manager immediately. The team has not announced any interim manager, but several candidates have been mentioned as potential replacements. The Red Sox will look to turn their season around and make a push for the playoffs.
