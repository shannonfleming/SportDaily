**Jerry Jones has adopted a unique approach to the upcoming NFL Draft, choosing to receive trade calls from other teams rather than making the calls himself.** 
## Context / Background
The 2026 NFL Draft is just around the corner, and teams are finalizing their strategies. Jerry Jones, the owner of the Dallas Cowboys, has revealed his approach to potential trades during the draft.
