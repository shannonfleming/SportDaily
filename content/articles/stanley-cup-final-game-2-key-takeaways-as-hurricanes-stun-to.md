## Context / Background
The Stanley Cup Final series is one of the most highly anticipated events in the NHL calendar, with the top teams from the Eastern and Western Conferences competing for the coveted trophy. This year, the Carolina Hurricanes and the Vegas Golden Knights are vying for the championship title.
