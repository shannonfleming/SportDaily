**Chelsea boss Sonia Bompastor has slammed VAR after Katie McCabe avoided a red card for pulling Alyssa Thompson's hair in their Champions League game against Arsenal.**
## Context / Background
The incident occurred during Chelsea's Champions League match against Arsenal, where Katie McCabe pulled Alyssa Thompson's hair, but the referee did not give her a red card. Sonia Bompastor, the Chelsea boss, was furious with the decision and criticized the VAR system.
