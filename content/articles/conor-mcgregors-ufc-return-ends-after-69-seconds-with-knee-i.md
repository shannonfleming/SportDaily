**Conor McGregor's highly anticipated return to the UFC ended in disappointment after just 69 seconds, as he suffered a knee injury in his fight against Max Holloway at UFC 329.**

## Context / Background
The fight was one of the most highly anticipated in recent UFC history, with McGregor looking to make a statement in his return to the octagon. However, disaster struck early in the first round when McGregor suffered a knee injury, forcing him to withdraw from the fight.
