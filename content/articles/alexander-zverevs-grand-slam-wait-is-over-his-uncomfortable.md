**Alexander Zverev has finally ended his wait for a Grand Slam title, winning the French Open in a thrilling five-set match.**

## Context / Background
The German tennis player had been seeking his maiden Grand Slam title for several years, and his victory at the French Open marks a significant milestone in his career. Zverev defeated Flavio Cobolli in the final, overcoming a tough challenge to emerge victorious.
