**Pep Guardiola has informed Manchester City players that he will be leaving the club at the end of the season, with the club already lining up Enzo Maresca as his potential replacement.**

## Context / Background
Pep Guardiola's departure from Manchester City has been a topic of discussion for some time, with the club preparing for life after the Spanish manager. Guardiola has been instrumental in transforming Manchester City into a dominant force in English football, winning numerous titles and establishing a distinct playing style.
