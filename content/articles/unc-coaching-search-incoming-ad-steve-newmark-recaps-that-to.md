**Executive Summary**: The University of North Carolina has begun its search for a new men's basketball coach after the firing of Hubert Davis. Incoming Athletic Director Steve Newmark has provided insight into the timeline that led to this decision.

## Context / Background
The University of North Carolina has been undergoing significant changes in its athletic department, with Steve Newmark set to take over as the new Athletic Director. One of his first major decisions was the firing of men's basketball coach Hubert Davis. This move has set off a coaching search, with several candidates already being considered for the position.

## Key Quote
> 'The decision to part ways with Hubert Davis was not taken lightly, but we believe it is in the best interest of the program moving forward.' - Steve Newmark, Incoming Athletic Director

## What's Next?
The search for a new coach is expected to be thorough and could take several weeks. In the meantime, the university will continue to support its student-athletes and prepare for the upcoming season. The new coach will be expected to hit the ground running, with the goal of returning the program to its former glory.
