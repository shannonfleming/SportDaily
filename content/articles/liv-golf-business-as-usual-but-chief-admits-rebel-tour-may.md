**LIV Golf is facing significant financial uncertainty as its chief executive admits the rebel tour may need to raise additional funds to sustain itself.**

## Context / Background
LIV Golf has been making headlines in the golfing world with its lucrative offers to top players, but the tour's financial stability has been a subject of concern. The Saudi-backed tour has been pumping millions into the sport, but there are doubts about whether this funding will continue in the long term.

> The LIV Golf chief executive has stated that it is 'business as usual' for the tour, but the uncertainty surrounding its funding has raised concerns among players and fans alike.

## What's Next?
The future of LIV Golf remains uncertain, with the tour's leadership exploring options to secure additional funding. The outcome of these efforts will have a significant impact on the sport, and golf fans around the world will be watching closely.
