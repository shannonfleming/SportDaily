**NBA officials have explained the reasoning behind the new draft lottery system, which aims to curb tanking in the league.**
## Context / Background
The NBA has been facing issues with tanking, where teams intentionally lose games to secure a higher draft pick. The new system aims to address this problem by expanding the lottery and changing the odds of teams getting certain picks.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The new system will feature a larger pool of teams in the lottery, with the top four teams having an equal chance of getting the first pick. The odds of getting certain picks will also be adjusted to discourage tanking.

> The new system is designed to promote competitive balance and discourage teams from tanking, according to NBA officials.

## What's Next?
The new system will be implemented in the upcoming season, with the first draft lottery under the new system taking place in 2027.
