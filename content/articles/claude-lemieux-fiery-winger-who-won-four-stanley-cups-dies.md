    **Claude Lemieux, a renowned and fiery winger in the National Hockey League, has sadly passed away at the age of 60. Lemieux was a four-time Stanley Cup champion and is remembered for his fierce competitiveness on the ice.**

## Context and Background
Claude Lemieux had a storied career in the NHL, playing for several teams including the Montreal Canadiens, New Jersey Devils, and Colorado Avalanche. He was known for his tenacity and skill on the ice, which earned him numerous accolades and the respect of his peers.
