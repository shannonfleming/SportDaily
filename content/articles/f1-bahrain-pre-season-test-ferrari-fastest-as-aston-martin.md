**Executive Summary**: Ferrari has topped the F1 Bahrain pre-season test, with Charles Leclerc clocking the quickest time. Aston Martin, however, ended their running early due to unforeseen issues.

## Context / Background
The F1 Bahrain pre-season test is a crucial event for teams to fine-tune their cars and strategies before the start of the new season. This year's test has seen several teams making significant improvements, with Ferrari emerging as one of the strongest contenders.
