**Chelsea have reached a verbal agreement with Aston Villa for the transfer of Morgan Rogers in a record-breaking £117m deal.**

## Context / Background
The transfer window has seen significant activity, with top clubs vying for the best talent. Chelsea, under their current management, have been keen on strengthening their squad, and Morgan Rogers has emerged as a key target. Aston Villa, having developed Rogers into a promising player, have negotiated a substantial deal that reflects the player's potential.
