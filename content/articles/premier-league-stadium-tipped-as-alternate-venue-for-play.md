**The English Football League (EFL) is considering alternative venues for the Championship play-off final due to the ongoing Spygate scandal.**
## Context / Background
The Championship play-off final is scheduled to take place soon, but the controversy surrounding Southampton has put the event in jeopardy. The EFL has announced that they are exploring alternative venues, with a Premier League stadium being one of the options.
