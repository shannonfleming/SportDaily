**The Orlando Magic are finalizing a deal to hire San Antonio Spurs assistant Sean Sweeney as their next head coach, according to sources.**
## Context / Background
The Orlando Magic have been searching for a new head coach after parting ways with their previous coach. The team has been looking for a candidate who can bring a new perspective and lead the team to success. Sean Sweeney, an assistant coach with the San Antonio Spurs, has emerged as the top candidate for the job.

Sweeney's experience as an assistant coach with the Spurs has prepared him to take on the challenge of leading the Magic. His coaching style and philosophy are expected to bring a fresh approach to the team.
> The Magic are taking a risk by hiring a first-time head coach, but Sweeney's potential and upside make him an attractive candidate.
## What's Next?
The Magic are expected to announce Sweeney's hiring in the coming days. The team will then begin preparing for the upcoming season, with Sweeney at the helm. The Magic will look to improve on their record from last season and make a push for the playoffs.
