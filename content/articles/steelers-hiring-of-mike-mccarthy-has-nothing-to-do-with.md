The hiring of Mike McCarthy by the Steelers marks a new era for the team, and it will be interesting to see how he impacts the organization. McCarthy has a wealth of experience in the NFL, having previously coached the Green Bay Packers and Dallas Cowboys. He has a proven track record of success, having led the Packers to a Super Bowl victory in 2011. However, his tenure with the Cowboys was less successful, and he was ultimately let go after the 2019 season.

The Steelers' decision to hire McCarthy may be seen as a safe choice, given his experience and familiarity with the NFL.
