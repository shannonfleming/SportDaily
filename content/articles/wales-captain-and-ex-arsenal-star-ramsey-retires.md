**Aaron Ramsey, the former Wales captain and Arsenal midfielder, has announced his retirement from professional football at the age of 35.**
## Context / Background
Aaron Ramsey had a storied career, playing for Arsenal and Wales, among other teams. He was known for his skill on the field and his dedication to his teams.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
Ramsey's career spanned over two decades, with numerous achievements, including FA Cups and a Euro semi-final appearance with Wales.

## Key Quote
> 'Diolch Aaron Ramsey' - a statement from the Football Association of Wales, thanking Ramsey for his service to Welsh football.
