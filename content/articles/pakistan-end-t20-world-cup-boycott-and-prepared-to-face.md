**Pakistan have ended their proposed boycott of the T20 World Cup match against India and will play as scheduled this Sunday.**
## Context / Background
The T20 World Cup has seen its share of controversies, and the latest development is Pakistan's decision to end their proposed boycott of the match against India. This comes after Bangladesh's removal from the tournament, which seems to have played a role in Pakistan's change of heart.

## Tactical Key Points
The match between Pakistan and India is one of the most highly anticipated in the tournament, with both teams having a strong following and a history of intense rivalry. The fact that Pakistan have decided to play the match as scheduled is a welcome development for fans of the sport.

> The T20 World Cup is a platform for countries to come together and showcase their cricketing skills, and it's great to see Pakistan and India putting their differences aside to play the match.

## What's Next?
The match between Pakistan and India is scheduled to take place this Sunday, and fans can catch all the action live on Sky Sports. With the boycott now ended, attention turns to the match itself, and which team will come out on top.
