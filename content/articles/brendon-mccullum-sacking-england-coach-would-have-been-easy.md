**England's Decision to Retain McCullum as Coach Sparks Debate.**
## Context / Background
The England cricket team recently faced a disappointing loss in the Ashes series, leading to speculation about the future of coach Brendon McCullum. Despite the setback, the England and Wales Cricket Board (ECB) has decided to stand by McCullum, citing that sacking him would have been the 'easy thing to do'.
