**George Russell has expressed his frustration with the early-season issues plaguing Mercedes, feeling that they are disproportionately affecting his side of the garage.**

## Context / Background
The Mercedes team has been experiencing a range of issues in the early part of the season, with George Russell bearing the brunt of these problems. Despite the team's best efforts, Russell feels that he is being impacted more than his teammate Kimi Antonelli.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Driver | Issues |
| --- | --- |
| George Russell | Multiple technical issues, poor qualifying performances |
| Kimi Antonelli | Fewer technical issues, more consistent qualifying |

> I feel like all of Mercedes' early-season issues are coming on my side of the garage, it's frustrating but we're working to resolve them.

## What's Next?
The team will be looking to address these issues ahead of the next race, with Russell hoping for a more trouble-free weekend. With the season still in its early stages, there is plenty of time for Mercedes to turn things around and get back to competing at the sharp end of the grid.
