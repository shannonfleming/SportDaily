    ## Context / Background
    The Women's March Madness tournament has been filled with exciting matches, and Day 4 was no exception. Virginia, a lower-ranked team, pulled off a stunning upset against No. 2 seed Iowa, advancing to the Sweet 16 for the first time since 2000.
