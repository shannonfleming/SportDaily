**The FIFA World Cup is just 50 days away, and the last-minute ticket sales phase has reopened.**
## Context and Background
The World Cup, one of the most highly anticipated sporting events globally, is set to kick off soon. As the event approaches, FIFA has announced the reopening of the last-minute ticket sales phase. This move comes after concerns were raised about the slow sales of tickets for some matches, including the USMNT's opener against Paraguay.
