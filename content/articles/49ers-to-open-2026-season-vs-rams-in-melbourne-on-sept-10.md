**The San Francisco 49ers are set to open their 2026 season against the Los Angeles Rams in Melbourne, Australia, on September 10.**
## Context / Background
The NFL has announced its schedule for the upcoming season, with several teams set to play international games. The 49ers and Rams will be part of this initiative, facing off in Melbourne as part of the league's efforts to grow its global presence.

The 49ers and Rams will be looking to start their seasons on a high note, with the game in Melbourne set to be an exciting encounter. The Seahawks, meanwhile, will be hosting the first game of the season on September 9.
