**Cherie DeVaux has made history by becoming the first female trainer to win the Kentucky Derby with Golden Tempo.**
## Context / Background
The Kentucky Derby is one of the most prestigious horse racing events in the world. This year's event saw a historic moment with Cherie DeVaux's Golden Tempo winning the race after a late surge.
