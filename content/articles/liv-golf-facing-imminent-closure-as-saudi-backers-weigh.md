**LIV Golf is facing an uncertain future as reports emerge that its Saudi backers are considering withdrawing their funding.** 
## Context / Background
LIV Golf, a professional golf tour, has been facing financial pressures and speculation about its future. The tour, which is backed by Saudi Arabia's Public Investment Fund, has been trying to establish itself as a rival to the PGA Tour.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The potential withdrawal of funding by Saudi backers could have significant implications for LIV Golf, including the possibility of the tour's collapse. According to reports, the Saudi fund is under mounting financial pressures, which could lead to a reduction in its investment in LIV Golf.

> The future of LIV Golf is uncertain, and the potential withdrawal of Saudi funding could have far-reaching consequences for the sport.

## What's Next?
The future of LIV Golf remains uncertain, and it is unclear what the next steps will be. The tour's CEO has denied speculation about the tour's demise, but the potential withdrawal of Saudi funding has raised concerns about its viability.
