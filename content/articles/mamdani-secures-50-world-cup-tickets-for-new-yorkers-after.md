**New York City residents are set to benefit from a unique opportunity to purchase World Cup tickets at a significantly reduced price of $50, thanks to negotiations between Zohran Mamdani and FIFA's Gianni Infantino.**

## Context / Background
The World Cup, one of the most highly anticipated international sporting events, attracts millions of fans worldwide. However, the high cost of tickets often poses a significant barrier for many enthusiasts. In a bid to make the event more accessible, Zohran Mamdani has successfully negotiated with FIFA to secure a limited number of tickets at a discounted price of $50 for New York City residents.
