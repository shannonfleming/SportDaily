**Chloe Kim's bid for a historic third consecutive Olympic halfpipe gold medal has been thwarted by South Korea's Choi Ga-on.**

## Context / Background
Chloe Kim, the American snowboarding sensation, had been aiming to make history by becoming the first woman to win three consecutive Olympic halfpipe gold medals. However, her dreams were dashed by Choi Ga-on, who put in a stunning performance to take the top spot on the podium.
