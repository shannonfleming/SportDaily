**Everton has been told to pay Burnley £35m due to breaches of the Premier League's Profitability and Sustainability Regulations (PSR).**
## Context / Background
The Premier League's Profitability and Sustainability Regulations are in place to ensure that clubs operate within their means and do not accumulate excessive debt. Everton's breach of these regulations has resulted in a significant penalty.

| Club | Penalty Amount |
| --- | --- |
| Everton | £35m |

> No official statement has been released by Everton regarding the penalty, but it is expected that the club will face significant financial implications.

## What's Next?
The penalty is expected to have a significant impact on Everton's finances and may affect the club's ability to make transfers and investments in the upcoming season. The club will need to reassess its financial strategy to ensure compliance with the Premier League's regulations.
