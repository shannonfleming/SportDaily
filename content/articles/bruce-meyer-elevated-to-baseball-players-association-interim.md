**Bruce Meyer has been elevated to the position of interim executive director of the baseball players association.** 
## Context / Background
The decision comes after the resignation of Tony Clark, who stepped down due to an inappropriate relationship with his sister-in-law. This move has left the MLBPA 'devastated' and scrambling to find a new leader.

## Quote
> No official statement has been made by Bruce Meyer, but sources close to the matter have confirmed his appointment as interim executive director.
## What's Next?
The MLBPA will likely undergo a thorough search for a permanent executive director, while Bruce Meyer takes the reins in the interim.
