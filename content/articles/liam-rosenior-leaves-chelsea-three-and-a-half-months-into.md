**Liam Rosenior's tenure as Chelsea's head coach has come to an abrupt end, with the club announcing his departure just three and a half months into his contract.**

## Context / Background
Liam Rosenior was appointed as Chelsea's head coach in January, signing a contract that was set to run until 2032. However, his time in charge has been marked by a series of disappointing results, including five successive Premier League defeats.
