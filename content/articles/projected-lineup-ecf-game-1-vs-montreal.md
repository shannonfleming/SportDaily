**The Eastern Conference Finals are set to begin, with the Hurricanes facing off against the Montreal Canadiens in Game 1.**
## Context / Background
The Hurricanes are coming off an 11-day layoff, the longest in the NHL since 1919. This break has raised questions about whether it will help or hurt their chances in the playoffs.
