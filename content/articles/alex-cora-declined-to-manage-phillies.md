**Alex Cora has declined the offer to manage the Philadelphia Phillies, leaving the team to continue their search for a new manager.**

## Context / Background
The Phillies have been searching for a new manager since parting ways with Rob Thomson. Alex Cora, a seasoned manager with experience in the MLB, was considered a top candidate for the position. However, it appears that Cora has decided not to take the job, leaving the Phillies to explore other options.
