**Tiger Woods has announced that he will be stepping away from golf to seek treatment after his recent car crash.** 
## Context / Background
Tiger Woods, the renowned American professional golfer, has been involved in a car crash and has subsequently announced that he will be taking a break from golf. This decision comes after Woods was charged with a DUI, following the accident.
