**The Baltimore Ravens' attempt to acquire defensive end Maxx Crosby has fallen through, leaving General Manager Eric DeCosta 'gutted' over the failed trade.**

## Context / Background
The Ravens had been in talks to trade for Crosby, but the deal ultimately did not materialize. This development has left the team's front office disappointed, with DeCosta expressing his frustration over the failed trade.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table

The Ravens did manage to sign defensive end Trey Hendrickson, but the failure to acquire Crosby has raised questions about the team's offseason strategy.

## Key Quote
> 'We were close, but it didn't happen. I'm gutted that it didn't work out.' - Eric DeCosta, Ravens GM

## What's Next?
The Ravens will now have to regroup and reassess their roster needs. With the NFL free agency period still ongoing, the team may explore other options to bolster their defensive line.
