**Andy Robertson has announced that he will be leaving Liverpool at the end of the current season, bringing an end to his successful stint with the Reds.**

## Context / Background
Andy Robertson has been a crucial part of Liverpool's success in recent years, including their Champions League and Premier League title wins. The Scottish left-back has been praised for his tireless work rate, defensive skills, and attacking threat from the flank.
