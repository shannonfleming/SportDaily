**Spectators were delayed entering The Players ahead of the third round at TPC Sawgrass, following a fatal shooting close to the course the previous evening.**
## Context / Background
The incident occurred near the TPC Sawgrass golf course, where The Players tournament is being held. As a result, security measures were heightened, causing delays for fans trying to enter the venue.

## What's Next?
The third round of The Players will proceed with increased security measures in place. Fans are advised to arrive early and be patient with the enhanced security protocols.
