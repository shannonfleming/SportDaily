**Fernando Alonso believes that with the expertise of Adrian Newey, Aston Martin will eventually have the best car in F1, despite anticipating a challenging start to the 2026 season.**

## Context / Background
Aston Martin has been making significant strides in Formula 1, with the addition of Adrian Newey to their team being a major factor in their anticipated success. Fernando Alonso, a seasoned F1 driver, has expressed his confidence in the team's ability to dominate the sport in the future.

## Tactical Key Points
Alonso's statement highlights the team's long-term strategy and their focus on development. With Newey on board, Aston Martin is expected to make significant improvements to their car, which could potentially give them an edge over their competitors.

> Alonso is in no doubt that Aston Martin will eventually have the best car in F1, but acknowledges that they will be on the back foot in the first part of the 2026 season.

## What's Next?
As the 2026 F1 season approaches, all eyes will be on Aston Martin to see how they perform. With Alonso's confidence in the team and Newey's expertise, fans are eagerly awaiting the start of the season to see if Aston Martin can live up to their expectations.
