**Nottingham Forest have parted ways with manager Sean Dyche after a disappointing run of results, marking the club's third managerial departure this season.**

## Context / Background
Nottingham Forest's decision to sack Sean Dyche comes as a result of the team's poor performance in the Premier League. With only 17 matches under his belt, Dyche's tenure was cut short due to the club's low standing in the league table.

## What's Next?
The sacking of Sean Dyche leaves Nottingham Forest searching for a new manager to take the reins and turn the team's fortunes around. The club will be looking to make a swift appointment to minimize the disruption to the current season.
