**Texas has made history by becoming the first team to go from the First Four to the Sweet 16 in the NCAA Men's Basketball Tournament, defeating Gonzaga 74-68.**

## Context / Background
The NCAA Men's Basketball Tournament, also known as March Madness, is one of the most prestigious college basketball competitions in the United States. This year's tournament has seen several upsets, with Texas being one of the most surprising teams to advance to the Sweet 16.
