**Hearts have taken a significant step towards winning the Scottish Premiership title with a 2-1 win over Hibernian.**
## Context / Background
The Scottish Premiership has been a closely contested league this season, with several teams vying for the top spot. Hearts and Celtic have been the top two teams for most of the season, but Hearts' recent win over Hibernian has given them a three-point lead.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Team | Points | Goal Difference |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hearts | 63 | 15 |
| Celtic | 60 | 12 |
| Hibernian | 45 | -2 |
Hearts' win over Hibernian was a significant one, as it came against a team that was reduced to nine men. Blair Spittal scored the winning goal in the 86th minute to seal the victory for Hearts.

> Hearts' manager was pleased with the performance of his team, but acknowledged that there is still a long way to go in the season.

## What's Next?
Hearts will look to build on their lead at the top of the table with their next match against Aberdeen. Celtic, on the other hand, will face Rangers in a crucial match that could have a significant impact on the title race.
