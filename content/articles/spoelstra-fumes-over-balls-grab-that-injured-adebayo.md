   **Executive Summary**: Miami Heat coach Erik Spoelstra is furious over the play that injured Bam Adebayo, stating that LaMelo Ball should have been ejected from the game.

## Context / Background
The incident occurred during a recent NBA play-in game between the Miami Heat and the Charlotte Hornets. LaMelo Ball's grab on Bam Adebayo led to an injury that forced Adebayo out of the game.
