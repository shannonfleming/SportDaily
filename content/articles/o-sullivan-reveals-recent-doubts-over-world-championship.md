**Ronnie O'Sullivan has revealed he had doubts about participating in the World Snooker Championship prior to his impressive run at the World Open.**

## Context / Background
Ronnie O'Sullivan, known as 'The Rocket', has been a dominant figure in professional snooker for many years. His recent performance at the World Open has been impressive, reaching the final on Sunday. However, it has come to light that O'Sullivan had reservations about entering this year's World Snooker Championship.
