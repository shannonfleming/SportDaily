**Aston Martin's delayed development of their 2026 Formula 1 car is expected to hinder their performance in the early stages of the season.**

## Context / Background
Aston Martin has unveiled their new car, the AMR26, in Saudi Arabia, with Technical Director Adrian Newey acknowledging the team's late start. The delayed development is attributed to the complexities of designing an 'extreme' new car.

## Tactical Key Points
The team's late start on their 2026 challenger is likely to impact their competitiveness, particularly in the initial stages of the season. Despite this, the team remains optimistic about their chances, citing the car's innovative design.

> Adrian Newey stated that the team's late start is 'bound to' affect their results, but expressed confidence in the car's potential.

## What's Next?
Aston Martin will look to overcome their slow start and make the most of the upcoming season. The team's performance will be closely watched, with fans and pundits eager to see how they fare against their competitors.
