**MLB has issued suspensions to several players involved in a recent brawl between the Nationals and Red Sox.**
## Context / Background
The incident occurred during a game between the two teams, resulting in a benches-clearing brawl. The league has taken disciplinary action against the players involved.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Player | Team | Suspension Length |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cade Cavalli | Nationals | 7 games |
| Willson Contreras | Red Sox | 7 games |
## Key Quote
> "The league has made it clear that this type of behavior will not be tolerated." 
## What's Next? 
The suspended players will serve their suspensions immediately, unless they choose to appeal. The teams will need to adjust their lineups accordingly.
