**A statue of baseball legend Ichiro Suzuki was unveiled in Seattle, but the ceremony was marred by an unfortunate incident: the bat in the statue broke off.**

## Context / Background
The statue was unveiled at T-Mobile Park, home of the Seattle Mariners, as a tribute to Ichiro Suzuki's illustrious career. Suzuki is a highly revered figure in baseball, known for his impressive skills on the field and his dedication to the sport.
