**UFC Freedom 250 has garnered significant attention with its recent event at the White House, though its viewership fell short of Super Bowl numbers.**

The UFC Freedom 250 event was highly anticipated, with many speculating it could reach Super Bowl-type viewership numbers. However, according to recent reports, it averaged around 7 million viewers in the U.S., which, although impressive, did not quite match the Super Bowl's audience.
