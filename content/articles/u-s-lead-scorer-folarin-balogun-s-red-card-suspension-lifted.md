**The US soccer team has received a significant boost with the news that lead scorer Folarin Balogun's red card suspension has been lifted.**
## Context / Background
The decision comes after former US President Donald Trump reportedly called the FIFA president, leading to a review of the suspension. The news has sparked a political storm around the World Cup, with many questioning the influence of Trump's intervention.

| US | Folarin Balogun | 1 |
The lifting of the suspension means that Balogun will be available to play in the team's upcoming matches, including the crucial game against Belgium.
> The US team's chances of advancing in the tournament have increased significantly with Balogun's reinstatement.
## What's Next?
The US team will face Belgium in their next match, and with Balogun back in the lineup, they will be looking to secure a crucial win. The match is expected to be highly competitive, with both teams fighting for a spot in the next round.
