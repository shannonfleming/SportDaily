**Senegal has vowed to appeal the decision to strip them of their Africa Cup of Nations title.** 
## Context / Background
The Confederation of African Football (CAF) recently made the decision to award the 2025 Africa Cup of Nations title to Morocco, stripping Senegal of their title. This decision has been met with widespread criticism and outrage from the Senegalese government and football fans across the continent.

## What's Next?
