**Manchester United has taken a significant step towards building a new stadium, with the location for the proposed 100,000-seater ground revealed.**

The plans for the new stadium have been in the works for some time, with the club aiming to replace the current Old Trafford stadium. The new stadium is expected to be one of the largest in the UK, with a seating capacity of 100,000.

The new stadium is expected to feature state-of-the-art facilities, including advanced seating, improved concourses, and enhanced hospitality areas. The stadium will also be designed with sustainability in mind, incorporating green technologies and energy-efficient systems.
