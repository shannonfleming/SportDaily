**The search for a new England Test coach is underway following Brendon McCullum's departure, with several key tasks awaiting the successful candidate.**

## Context / Background
England's red-ball team is at a crossroads after the sacking of Brendon McCullum. The new coach will face a number of challenges, including finding a new captain and replacing the retiring Ben Stokes.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The next England Test coach will need to address several areas, including:
* Finding a new captain to lead the team
* Replacing Ben Stokes in the batting lineup
* Cutting out the 'boozing escapades' that have plagued the team in recent years

> The new coach will need to be able to make tough decisions and have a clear vision for the team's future.

## What's Next?
The new coach will have a busy schedule ahead of them, with a number of Test matches and series to prepare for. They will need to hit the ground running and make an immediate impact.
