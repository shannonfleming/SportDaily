**The Premier League has announced the launch of its new streaming service, Premflix, which will screen all Premier League matches live in Singapore.**
## Context / Background
The Premier League has been exploring ways to expand its reach and provide more access to its matches for fans around the world. With the launch of Premflix, the league is taking a significant step towards achieving this goal. The service will be available only in Singapore, but it is expected to pave the way for similar services in other countries.

> The launch of Premflix is a significant development for the Premier League and its fans in Singapore. It will provide unprecedented access to live matches and enhance the overall fan experience.

## What's Next? (Schedule)
The Premflix service is expected to launch in time for the 2026-27 Premier League season. Fans in Singapore can look forward to watching all Premier League matches live, including those played at 3 pm on Saturdays, which were previously subject to a blackout.
