    **Aaron Rodgers, the veteran quarterback of the Pittsburgh Steelers, has announced his plans to retire after the 2026 season, bringing an end to his 22-year career in the NFL.**
    
    ## Context / Background
    Rodgers, who is 42 years old, has been one of the most successful quarterbacks in the league, with numerous awards and accolades to his name. He has played for the Green Bay Packers and the New York Jets before joining the Steelers.

    > 'This is it, I'm done after this season. I've thought about it a lot, and I'm at peace with my decision.' - Aaron Rodgers
    
    ## What's Next?
    The Steelers will look to make the most of Rodgers' final season, with a strong roster and a good chance of making the playoffs. Rodgers' retirement will be a significant loss for the team, but they will have time to plan for the future.
