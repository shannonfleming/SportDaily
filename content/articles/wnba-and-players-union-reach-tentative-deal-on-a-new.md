**The WNBA has reached a tentative deal with its players' union on a new collective bargaining agreement, marking a significant step forward for the league and its players.**

## Context / Background
The WNBA and its players' union have been in negotiations for a new collective bargaining agreement for several months. The talks have focused on issues such as revenue sharing, player compensation, and benefits.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
Some of the key points of the tentative agreement include:
* Increased revenue sharing for players
* Improved player compensation and benefits
* Enhanced support for players' mental and physical health

## Key Quote
> The new labor deal is a significant step forward for the WNBA and its players, and it will help to ensure the long-term success and growth of the league.

## What's Next?
The tentative agreement will now be put to a vote by the players' union, and if approved, it will become the new collective bargaining agreement for the WNBA.
