The controversy surrounding Pierre Gasly's reinstatement to the Monaco Grand Prix podium has sparked a heated debate within the Formula 1 community. The initial decision to reinstate Gasly was met with criticism from several teams, including McLaren and Red Bull, who have now decided to take formal action.
