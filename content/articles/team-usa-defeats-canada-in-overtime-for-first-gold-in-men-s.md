**The United States men's hockey team has made history by winning their first Olympic gold medal since the 'Miracle on Ice' in 1980.**
## Context / Background
The US team, led by key players such as Jack Hughes and Connor Hellebuyck, faced off against Canada in the gold medal match. The game went into overtime, where the US emerged victorious.
