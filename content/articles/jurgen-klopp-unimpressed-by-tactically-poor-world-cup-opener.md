**Jurgen Klopp has expressed his disappointment with the tactics employed in the World Cup opener, labeling them as 'tactically poor'.** 
## Context / Background
The World Cup has kicked off, with Mexico securing a 2-0 win over South Africa in their opening match. However, the victory was marred by three players being sent off, raising concerns about the team's discipline.
