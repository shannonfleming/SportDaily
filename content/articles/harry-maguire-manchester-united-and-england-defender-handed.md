**Harry Maguire, the Manchester United and England defender, has been handed a 15-month suspended sentence after a guilty verdict over the Mykonos incident in 2020.**

## Context / Background
The incident occurred in 2020 when Maguire was involved in an altercation on the Greek island of Mykonos. He was initially found guilty and sentenced, but the case was retried, resulting in the same verdict.

## What's Next?
The sentence is suspended, which means Maguire will not have to serve time in prison unless he commits another offense. Manchester United and the English Football Association have not commented on the verdict, but it is expected that they will release a statement soon.
