**The University of Connecticut (UConn) has advanced to the NCAA men's basketball national title game after defeating the University of Illinois 71-62 in the Final Four.**
## Context / Background
UConn's victory over Illinois marks their third trip to the national title game in four years. The team's strong performance has been a highlight of the tournament, with their ability to adapt to different opponents being a key factor in their success.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Team | Score |
| --- | --- |
| UConn | 71 |
| Illinois | 62 |
UConn's win was fueled by their consistent offense and stout defense, which held Illinois to a lower score than expected.
> UConn's coach noted that the team's experience and chemistry played a significant role in their victory.
## What's Next?
UConn will face the winner of the Arizona vs. Michigan game in the national title game. The team is looking to capitalize on their momentum and bring home the championship.
