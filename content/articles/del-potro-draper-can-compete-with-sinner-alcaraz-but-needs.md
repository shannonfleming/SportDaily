**Juan Martin del Potro believes Jack Draper has the potential to compete with top tennis players like Jannik Sinner and Carlos Alcaraz, but notes that Draper needs to have confidence in his body.**

## Context / Background
Juan Martin del Potro, a former Grand Slam champion, has been watching Jack Draper's progress and is impressed with the young player's skills. Del Potro thinks that Draper has the potential to compete with the best players in the world, including Sinner and Alcaraz.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
Draper's recent performances have shown that he has the talent and dedication required to succeed at the highest level. However, he needs to work on his physical fitness and mental toughness to compete with the top players.

> Draper needs "confidence in his body" to compete with Sinner and Alcaraz, according to Del Potro.

## What's Next? (Schedule)
Draper's upcoming matches will be crucial in determining his progress and whether he can live up to Del Potro's expectations. Fans will be watching closely to see how he performs against top opponents.
