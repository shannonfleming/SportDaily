**NBA Commissioner Adam Silver has expressed concerns over the league's ongoing issue with tanking, stating that current efforts to prevent it are 'not working'.**
## Context / Background
The NBA has been struggling with the issue of tanking, where teams intentionally lose games to gain better draft picks. Despite attempts to address the problem, Silver believes that the incentives for tanking remain misaligned.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The NBA has tried various methods to discourage tanking, including changes to the draft lottery system. However, teams continue to find ways to exploit the system and prioritize draft picks over winning games.
> Adam Silver has acknowledged that the league needs to find new solutions to the problem, considering all possible remedies.
## What's Next?
The NBA will continue to explore ways to address the issue of tanking, with Silver promising to look into all options. The league's efforts to stop tanking will be closely watched, as fans and teams alike hope for a more competitive and fair season.
