**Pep Guardiola insists the Premier League title race is far from over, despite Manchester City's recent struggles with consistency, creativity, and the goal-scoring drought of Erling Haaland.**

## Context / Background
The Premier League has seen a tightly contested title race this season, with several teams vying for the top spot. Manchester City, under the guidance of Pep Guardiola, has been one of the favorites to win the title. However, recent performances have raised concerns about the team's ability to maintain the consistency required to secure the championship.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
Manchester City's recent performances have been marred by a lack of creativity and goal-scoring opportunities. The team's reliance on Erling Haaland's goals has been evident, and his current drought has significantly impacted the team's overall performance.

> The Premier League title race is not over, and we will continue to fight for every point.

## What's Next? 
Manchester City's upcoming fixtures will be crucial in determining their chances of winning the title. The team needs to regain its consistency and find ways to score goals without relying heavily on Erling Haaland. The fans will be eager to see how Guardiola's tactics evolve in response to the current challenges.
