**The recent trade of Myles Garrett from the Browns to the Rams has sent shockwaves through the NFL, with the details of the conditional pick now emerging.**
## Context / Background
The trade, which was finalized recently, saw the Browns receive Jared Verse and three picks, including a conditional third-round pick that could become a first-round pick if the Rams trade Garrett to an AFC North team.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The implications of this trade are significant, with the Rams bolstering their defensive line with the addition of Garrett. The Browns, on the other hand, have acquired a talented young player in Verse and have added to their draft capital.

> The Browns' conditional third-round pick becoming a first-round pick if the Rams trade Garrett to an AFC North team adds an extra layer of complexity to the deal.

## What's Next?
As the NFL season approaches, all eyes will be on the Rams and how they integrate Garrett into their lineup. The Browns, meanwhile, will be looking to rebuild and make the most of their draft picks.
