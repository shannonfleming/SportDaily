**England cricketers Ben Stokes and Gus Atkinson have been cleared of blame in a recent nightclub incident.**

## Context / Background
The incident in question occurred at a nightclub, where Stokes and Atkinson were involved in an altercation. The England and Wales Cricket Board (ECB) conducted an investigation into the matter.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Player | Incident | Outcome |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ben Stokes | Nightclub altercation | Cleared of blame |
| Gus Atkinson | Nightclub altercation | Cleared of blame |

> The ECB has concluded that Ben Stokes and Gus Atkinson were not to blame for the violent conduct during the nightclub incident.

## What's Next?
Stokes has returned as England captain, and both he and Atkinson are set to join the England Men's Test squad for the upcoming match at Trent Bridge.
