**Jannik Sinner's French Open campaign came to an abrupt end after he suffered from severe cramp during his second-round match.**
## Context / Background
The Italian tennis player was two sets up and a game away from winning the match when he began to experience cramp. Despite his best efforts, Sinner was unable to recover and eventually lost the match.
