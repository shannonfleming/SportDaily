**The Baltimore Ravens have cancelled their trade agreement for defensive end Maxx Crosby with the Las Vegas Raiders due to medical concerns.**

## Context / Background
The trade between the Ravens and the Raiders for Maxx Crosby was highly anticipated but has now fallen through due to Crosby's failed physical. This development has significant implications for both teams, particularly in terms of their defensive strategies for the upcoming season.

## What's Next?
The cancellation of the Maxx Crosby trade leaves both the Ravens and the Raiders to reassess their roster needs. For the Ravens, finding an alternative defensive end who can contribute immediately will be a priority. The Raiders, meanwhile, will need to consider other options for trading or utilizing Maxx Crosby, taking into account his medical status.
