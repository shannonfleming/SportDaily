**Kimi Antonelli has made history by becoming the youngest F1 championship leader after winning the Japanese Grand Prix at Suzuka.**

## Context / Background
The Japanese Grand Prix was a highly anticipated event, with top drivers competing for the top spot. Kimi Antonelli, in particular, was a favorite to win, given his impressive performance in previous races.
