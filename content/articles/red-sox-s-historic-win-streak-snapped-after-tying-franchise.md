**The Red Sox's historic win streak has come to an end after the team tied their franchise record of 15 consecutive wins.**
## Context / Background
The Red Sox had been on a tear, winning 15 games in a row and tying their franchise record. However, their streak was snapped by the Orioles in their latest matchup.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Team | Wins | Losses |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Red Sox | 15 | 0 |
| Orioles | 1 | 0 |
The Red Sox had been playing exceptional baseball, with their offense and pitching both contributing to their success. However, the Orioles were able to capitalize on some mistakes and come out on top.
> The Red Sox's win streak was a testament to their dedication and hard work. Although it has come to an end, the team is still in a great position to make a deep playoff run.
## What's Next?
The Red Sox will look to bounce back from their loss and start a new win streak. They will face the Orioles again in their next matchup, looking to come out on top and regain their momentum.
