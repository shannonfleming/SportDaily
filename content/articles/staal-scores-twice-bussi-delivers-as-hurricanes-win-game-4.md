**The Carolina Hurricanes have evened up the Stanley Cup Final series at 2-2 with a win in Game 4.**
## Context / Background
The Stanley Cup Final is the championship series of the National Hockey League (NHL), with the Carolina Hurricanes facing off against the Vegas Golden Knights. The series is now tied at 2-2, with each team having won two games.
## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
| Team | Goals | Shots |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hurricanes | 4 | 30 |
| Golden Knights | 3 | 25 |
The Hurricanes' Jordan Staal scored twice in the game, including an acrobatic backhand shot that pulled the team even with the Golden Knights.
## Key Quote
> 'We're just taking it one game at a time, trying to focus on our own game and not get too high or too low,' said Hurricanes coach Rod Brind'Amour.
## What's Next?
The series will continue with Game 5, as the Hurricanes and Golden Knights battle for the Stanley Cup championship.
