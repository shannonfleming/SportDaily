**The 2025-26 NBA season is nearing its end, and the league has announced the finalists for its major awards.** 
## Context / Background
The NBA awards are given annually to recognize the best players, coaches, and executives in the league. The finalists for the Most Valuable Player, Defensive Player of the Year, Rookie of the Year, Sixth Man of the Year, Coach of the Year, and Most Improved Player awards have been announced.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table

> The NBA awards are a great way to recognize the hard work and dedication of the players, coaches, and executives in the league.

The winners of the NBA awards will be announced at a later date. The league will also announce the winners of the other awards, including the Sixth Man of the Year, Coach of the Year, and Most Improved Player awards.
