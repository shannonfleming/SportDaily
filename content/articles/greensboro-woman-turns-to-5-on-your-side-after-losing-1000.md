**A Greensboro woman has lost $1,000 to a Stanley Cup ticket scam, prompting warnings for fans to be cautious when purchasing tickets.**

## Context / Background
The Stanley Cup Final is one of the most highly anticipated events in the NHL, with fans eager to secure tickets to witness their favorite teams in action. However, with the high demand for tickets comes the risk of scams, as seen in the recent case of a Greensboro woman who lost $1,000 to a fake ticket seller.
