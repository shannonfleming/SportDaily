**Wyndham Clark has taken a commanding four-shot lead at the US Open after the first round at Shinnecock Hills.**
## Context / Background
The US Open is one of the most prestigious golf tournaments in the world, and this year's edition is being held at the challenging Shinnecock Hills course. The first round was affected by rain, but Wyndham Clark was able to navigate the conditions and take the lead.
