**MLB has issued a warning to several San Francisco Giants pitchers who wrote Bible verses on their caps during Pride Night, sparking controversy and debate.**
## Context / Background
The incident occurred during the Giants' Pride Night celebration, where players wore special edition caps to show support for the LGBTQ+ community. However, some pitchers decided to add their own personal touch by writing Bible verses on their caps, which was seen as a gesture of protest against the Pride Night celebration.

## What's Next?
The Giants organization has yet to comment on the incident, but it is expected that the team will address the issue in the coming days. The MLB has also announced that it will be reviewing its uniform policy to prevent similar incidents in the future.
