This plea comes ahead of the highly anticipated final against Arsenal, where Guardiola's team will be without the services of Marc Guehi due to the current rules. The rule in question states that players who have played for another team in the same competition are ineligible to play for a different team in the same season. In Guehi's case, he played for Chelsea's U21 team in the EFL Trophy, which is the same competition as the Carabao Cup, albeit a different division.
